Featured Session: The Guardian in Conversation with Mahmoud Khalil on the Cost of Dissent

Event ID: PP1149185

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon 1-4 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

When Mahmoud Khalil was targeted and nearly deported by the Trump administration for his advocacy, his life as a Palestinian student and soon-to-be father were irrevocably upended. Now released from detention – but still facing the threat of deportation – Khalil joins The Guardian for an unflinching conversation on his ordeal, the system that tried to silence him, and the personal and political stakes of resistance. *Mahmoud Khalil will be participating in this session virtually
