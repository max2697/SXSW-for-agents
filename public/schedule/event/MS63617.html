Summary

Ras Mundi is an international reggae band from Austin, TX that incorporates elements of hip-hop and rock & roll creating a conscious message of culture and unity. Up from humble roots, Ras Mundi currently consists of nine members working together to create a massive sound to heal the soul and the nation. Their sound resonates vocal harmonies, heavy percussion, melodic keys, thick baselines, and trumpet… with lyrics chanting down the Babylon System and resurrecting the movement of Jah people! Ras Mundi has shared the stage with reggae luminaries The Wailers featuring Junior Marvin, Third World, and Michael Black. Ras Mundi hopes to accomplish big ‘TINGS in 2026 that includes playing the SXSW Music Festival, as well as releasing a full-length album of original material. One love.
