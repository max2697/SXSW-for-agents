The Last Real Circus

Event ID: MS64021

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 11:20 PM - 11:50 PM

Venue: Lamberts

View on Official SXSW

Summary

From Richmond, VA, Jason Farlow & The Last Real Circus craft a raw, soulful blend of Americana and Indie Rock. The project is a musical collective centered on singer-songwriter Jason Farlow, renowned for his energetic stage presence, heartfelt storytelling, and musicianship. Touring both solo and with his band, Farlow has captivated a loyal following with heartbeat singles like “Nottoway” and “Gypsy Woman.” His sound channels a wide range of influences, from Tom Petty and Joe Cocker to Kings of Leon and Alabama Shakes. Their rising trajectory has earned them opening slots for an impressive roster of artists, including Jon Russell of The Head and The Heart, Band of Heathens, Blues Traveler, Nicole Atkins, Bones of JR Jones, Devon Gilfillian, Tim Reynolds, Biz Markie, The Sleepwalkers and many others.
