Tavo Rayo

Event ID: MS64219

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 1:45 AM - 2:00 AM

Venue: Hotel Vegas at Volstead (Hotel Vegas)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Tavo Rayo, a south Texas native rising star in the reggaetón and perreo scene, blends infectious rhythms with magnetic stage energy to captivate audiences across the U.S. and Latin America. Known for his dynamic flow and catchy hooks, he’s rapidly gaining momentum carving out a space as a fresh voice in urban Latin music. With roots deeply planted in the perreo tradition, Tavo Rayo’s sound is a vibrant fusion of old-school reggaetón beats and modern urbano influences, appealing to both traditional fans and a new generation of listeners. His recent singles have garnered notable playlist placements and rave reviews for their authenticity and dancefloor-ready vibe. Tavo’s live performances are a high-energy experience, blending charismatic interaction with an electrifying presence that commands the stage and ignites crowd participation. As he continues to expand his footprint bringing authentic Latin flavor, undeniable talent, and an engaging performance that leaves audiences wanting more.
