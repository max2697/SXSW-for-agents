Leadership Unblocked: Break Through What's Holding You Back

Event ID: PP1162336

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Ever feel like you’re doing all the “right” things—yet still feel stuck? The problem isn’t effort or ambition. It’s the unseen beliefs shaping how you show up at work. In this eye-opening talk, Harvard Business Press author and host of the Coaching Real Leaders podcast, Muriel M. Wilkins, pulls back the curtain on what holds even the highest achievers back. Drawing from her bestselling book, Leadership Unblocked, she reveals seven beliefs that limit leadership potential—and offers a practical roadmap to break through them and lead with greater impact and ease.
