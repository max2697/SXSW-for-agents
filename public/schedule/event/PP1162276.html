Summary

Ozempic revolutionized diabetes treatment, inspired by Gila monster venom - proving nature holds answers to health challenges. This session explores how unique organisms like snails and cavefish can improve our quality of life. Panelists discuss the most exciting ways curious critters, genetic engineering and biomimicry transform human health and society through nature-inspired discoveries that could change our future.
