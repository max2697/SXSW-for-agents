The Next AI Revolution: From Cloud to Local

Event ID: PP1162391

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Salon E (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

AI adoption is growing, but cloud-based AI is hitting limits like high costs, privacy concerns, environmental damage, & latency issues. This session will explore why the next generation of AI will be specialized, expert, & local. Panelists will unpack how on-device AI can deliver improved performance, privacy, & scalability with a more sustainable infrastructure. They’ll share how local AI is changing businesses, reshaping privacy expectations, and shifting the global tech landscape - and the next steps the industry has to take to make the use of local AI widespread.
