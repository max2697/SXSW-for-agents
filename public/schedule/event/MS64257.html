Summary

Electric Enemy is the alternative rock project of London-based multi-instrumentalist and three-time award-winning songwriter Jim Lawton. Fusing the raw power of Muse, Royal Blood and Foo Fighters with sharp social commentary and introspective lyricism, Electric Enemy deliver a high-energy sound built for modern rock stages. With 15M+ streams, multiple sold-out tours, #1 and #3 European iTunes Rock chart positions, eight weeks in Germany’s Top Rock Radio chart, and a #1 spot on TikTok’s GSA Rock playlist, the band has quickly established itself as a standout international live act. Renowned for their explosive performances, Electric Enemy were personally invited to support Jerry Cantrell on tour across Europe, with Lawton requested to duet on Cantrell’s iconic Rooster at the London Shepherd’s Bush Empire finale. Recent headline and showcase performances across Italy and Germany have further accelerated their global momentum. Outside the band, Lawton is an in-demand multi genre writer-producer for Universal-Sony + his music secured multiple sync placements with Netflix, ESPN, Disney+ WarnerBros+Gibson Guitars. “Is this the best live band in the UK”? - Sat Bisla - A & R Worldwide
