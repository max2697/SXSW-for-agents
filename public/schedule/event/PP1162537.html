Now is Japan's Time: Leading the Future with Deep Tech

Event ID: PP1162537

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Salon E (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Japan has a long history of developing fermentation technologies, which have served as the foundation for the growth of its biotechnology industry. Additionally, Japanese anime and games have garnered a large global fan base, generating excitement around new business opportunities utilizing these IPs. In this session, four speakers will share real-time insights from diverse perspectives on the forefront of Japan’s deep tech and IP businesses, as well as the emerging global ventures centered around them!
