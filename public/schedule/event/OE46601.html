Connecty Official SXSW Social: Ice Cream + Coffee + Community

Event ID: OE46601

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Venue: Cenote

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join Connecty for an Official SXSW Ice Cream + Coffee Social featuring Amy’s Ice Cream at Cenote. We’re turning one of East Austin’s dreamiest coffee houses into a neighborhood SXSW oasis. Whether you want a frothy latte, cool sugar and cream from the legendary Amy's, or a quick break from the hustle, come recharge with us at the indoor + outdoor patio space. <p> Why Stop By: <br> - Ice Cream + Coffee, yes please: Enjoy complimentary Amy’s Ice Cream and craft coffee from Cenote (on us!). A full service bar is also available for purchase. <br> - The Tech: Get an exclusive first look at Connecty, the new app designed to simplify IRL gatherings for hyper local communities. From smart event suggestions to custom contact labeling for your communities, check out how how we’re making it easier to organize everything from casual hangouts to large events. (New tech; patent pending). <br> - The Connection: We are seeking Connecty "Founding Members" - folks who are already organizing group events and looking for the right organization tool to do it. Tell us your hosting pain points, feature requests, and meet the team building the solution. <br> <p> Access & Entry: <br> - SXSW Badge Holders: Priority Access. Simply scan your badge at the door. <br> - Local Connectors (Non-Badge): We have a limited guest list reserved for community organizers, social chairs, and event hosts to come check out the app and chat about features wanted and the pain points of scheduling. (Subject to capacity). <br> <p> <p> Want a spot? Head to Instagram @connecty_official for instructions on how to RSVP. <p> <p> https://www.getconnecty.com/sxsw <p>
