The Rise of the Red Hot Chili Peppers: Our Brother, Hillel

Event ID: FS19549

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 9:15 PM - 10:48 PM

Venue: Alamo Lamar 6 (Alamo Lamar)

Summary

An in-depth look at the gritty formative years of the Red Hot Chili Peppers and the singular vision of original guitarist Hillel Slovak. Featuring interviews with bandmates Flea and Anthony Kiedis and others close to Slovak, the film explores the band’s early evolution on the LA music scene and the deep bond of their childhood friendship.
