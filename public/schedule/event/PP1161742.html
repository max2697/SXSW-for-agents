Let’s Talk Spanglish

Event ID: PP1161742

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Guadalupe (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Meet up with Spanish- and English-speaking peers to practice bilingualism in a welcoming, judgment-free space designed for educators. Participants will explore the power of linguistic diversity in learning environments, experience a low-stakes card-based activity that fosters authentic language use, and reflect on how social interaction supports language development. Attendees will leave with practical strategies to build inclusive, multilingual classrooms and communities.
