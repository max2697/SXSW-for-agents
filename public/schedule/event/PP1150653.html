Ethical & Unethical AI in Music

Event ID: PP1150653

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Capitol Ballroom E (Downright Austin)

Summary

Join Virginie Berger (Forbes) as she moderates a hard-hitting session with Kato On The Track, Michael MJ Jacob (CTO of BeatStars), and a leading industry voice to separate hype from harm. We’ll unpack real cases training data, rights and licensing and give creators the practical rules and tools they need to protect their work and profit from AI, not be erased by it.
