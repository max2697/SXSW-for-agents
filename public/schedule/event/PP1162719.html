The World’s Game, Our Stage: FOX Previews FIFA World Cup 2026

Event ID: PP1162719

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

This summer, the world’s greatest sporting event FIFA WORLD CUP returns to the U.S. for the first time in three decades. But this isn’t just any event, FIFA WORLD CUP 2026 promises to deliver a cultural moment that defines a generation. FOX Sports broadcasters and U.S. Soccer legends Alexi Lalas, Stu Holden and Carli Lloyd talk with acclaimed host and reporter Jenny Taft, reflecting on the evolution of the beautiful game in America and preview what’s in store for millions of viewers when FOX presents the biggest World Cup ever.
