Hsu Untied: Beyond Numbers: Decision-Making in the Math Classroom

Event ID: PP1161771

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 10:45 AM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 3 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join a live podcast recording that explores the dynamic relationship between math and decision-making. Our guests will examine how math instruction can nurture decision-making skills such as probabilistic thinking and numeracy, and how applying decision-making contexts can lead to new math structures that feel more meaningful and relevant. Through practical examples, the discussion will show how integrating these approaches helps students apply mathematical reasoning to real-world choices and develop lasting habits of thoughtful, evidence-based analysis.
