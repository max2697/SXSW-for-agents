How to Avoid Spreading Misinformation on Your Platform

Event ID: PP1162229

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

It’s no secret that today’s media landscape is more fractured than ever. As trust in legacy media declines, people are consuming news in less traditional ways and, instead, often turn to content creators for the latest news, commentary, and connection. But how can they balance influence and responsibility in such a fast-paced digital ecosystem? Join industry-leading creators and strategists in conversation to explore how, in a digital world driven by clicks and engagement, you can communicate responsibly online. We’ll dive into how to build and leverage community, recognize and preempt false narratives, and turn complex realities into sharable content while engaging your audience authentically.
