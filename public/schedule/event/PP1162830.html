Charmageddon: Robots That Steal Hearts and Jobs

Event ID: PP1162830

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Robots are no longer confined to factories. Today, they work in offices, live in homes, and support people in hospitals. But even the most advanced technical performance doesn’t ensure success. What often makes the difference? Likeability. In this talk, we explore how product strategy, design and character development help robots go from impressive machines to trusted companions. Through real-world case studies, we’ll show how emotional connection can be just as important as capability.
