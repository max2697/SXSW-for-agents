The Pickleball Payoff: Betting on America’s Top Sport

Event ID: PP1162553

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Major League Pickleball and the PPA Tour are transforming pickleball into America’s next major pro sport. With 311% growth and investors like LeBron James and Tom Brady, the game is booming. This session features voices from across the sport, including MLP team owner Gary Vaynerchuk, the world’s #1 player, and MLP’s commissioner, to explore league building, athlete branding, sponsorship, and media. Attendees will get a full-picture snapshot of pro pickleball’s rapid evolution.
