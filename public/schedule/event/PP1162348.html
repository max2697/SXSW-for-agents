Why Innovative Mental Health Benefits Are the Future of Work

Event ID: PP1162348

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon B (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Mental health is now a make-or-break factor in workforce productivity, retention, and culture. But traditional offerings - meditation apps, and EAPS - aren’t cutting it. This panel explores bold, evidence-backed solutions like ketamine-assisted therapy, stellate ganglion block, and emerging psychedelic treatments. Hear from leading researchers and forward-thinking employers who are reshaping mental health support to drive performance, reduce costs, and keep their people thriving.
