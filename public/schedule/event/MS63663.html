Summary

xBValentine is a queer Latina artist from Georgetown, Texas, unapologetically carving her own lane in the music world. As a first-generation creative, she’s spent over a decade breaking barriers from racking up accolades at open mics to sharing stages with names like Joyner Lucas, WESTSIDE Boogie, Snow Tha Product, Lupe Fiasco, & more. Known for her bold, genre-blending sound, XB’s music fuses hip-hop, R&B, and Latin influences, reflecting her diverse roots and fearless individuality. Her rise has been meteoric first going viral in 2020 with her LGBTQ+ anthem “Vibe With You,” surpassing a million streams across platforms. In 2023, she made waves again with Paul Wall on “LIKE DIS,” amassing over 10 million TikTok views. Most recently, her hit “Don’t Play With Me” featuring Amanda Perez exploded online, earning 2.2 billion TikTok views, nearing 1 million YouTube views, and surpassing a million streams across platforms. xB isn’t just a voice; she’s a movement.
