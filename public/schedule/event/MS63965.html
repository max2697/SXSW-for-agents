Summary

In March of 2023, Lili Hickman, Caelin Tralongo and Madison Baker, didn't realize what awaited them on the other end of a rough mix. After a single listen, what began as a collaboration on Jekyll & Hyde transformed into a full album and the budding of new band. Some may call it fate, others pure luck, but they call it Next of Kin. With a sound that blends country-tinged soundscapes, marked by rich three-part harmonies and honest storytelling, it's hard for any listener in any room not to be moved by this cult country trio currently taking Austin, TX by the horns-one venue at a time.
