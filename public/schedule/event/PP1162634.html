Growing Intelligence: Youth, AI, and the Future of Ag

Event ID: PP1162634

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

AI is revolutionizing the world around us, from how we grow food and educate our children, to how we deliver care – and it’s happening fast. So how do we ensure the next generation is empowered to thrive in an unknown future? Since 2017, 4-H and Google have equipped nearly 1.5M youth with the skills to succeed in an AI-powered world. Join this session to explore why equitable access to AI education and support for youth-led innovation are essential to building prosperity and resilience across communities and industries.
