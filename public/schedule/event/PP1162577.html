Artistic Disruption for Business Innovation

Event ID: PP1162577

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Rio Grande Ballroom 1 (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Unlock radical imagination with avant-garde creativity methods from Chicago’s Futurist Salon. In this interactive workshop for innovation and culture leaders, you’ll apply speculative avant-garde techniques—surrealist games (metaphor jamming, automatic writing), situationist exercises (dérive-style cultural mapping), and futurist scenario play—to reframe challenges, discover unseen connections, and spark breakthrough ideas. You’ll leave with hands-on tools and a simple framework for hosting your own salons that shift mental models and drive bold innovation across your organization.
