Zero-Gravity, Zero Barriers: Future of Inclusive Spaceflight

Event ID: PP1162561

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Room 203-204 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Discover what true Space accessibility means at this groundbreaking SXSW panel. Experts—including the first deaf Asian female pilot and aspiring astronaut, the founder of the Blind Institute of Technology, and a Space leader championing disability inclusion—reveal what it will take to make Space open to all. Explore innovations in pilot training, zero-gravity research, AI-assisted interfaces, and adaptive prosthetics. Learn how inclusive Space design inspires broader accessibility on Earth, reshaping our vision of who belongs in the final frontier.
