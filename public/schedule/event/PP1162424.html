Changing the Game: The Playbook for Creator-Driven Campaigns

Event ID: PP1162424

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Gaming is one of the largest forms of entertainment in the world & the creator economy within it is rapidly expanding. A process that once prioritized creators with the largest audiences & revenue streams has since evolved to working with a range of creators, targeting those with the most engaged audiences. As the industries continue to grow & scale each year, recruiting, managing, measuring & tracking hundreds of creators can feel impossible, but it doesn’t have to. This panel explores how brands & developers are building smart systems that fuel high-performing evergreen creator programs.
