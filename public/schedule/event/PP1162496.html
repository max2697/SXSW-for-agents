Creativity, Commerce & Chaos: Tech & Indie Filmmaking

Event ID: PP1162496

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Bridge Ballroom AB (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As AI transforms how we create and consume media, quiet revolutions are happening behind the scenes: creators, producers, agents, and technologists are building the infrastructure to track, protect, and monetize creativity across its entire lifecycle. This panel brings together a first-time filmmaker, a veteran distributor, a top agent, and an AI platform CEO to explore how attribution, metadata, royalty tracking, and ethical packaging are becoming essential to creative business.
