Pharma vs. Supplements: Breaking the False Health Dichotomy

Event ID: PP1162570

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Salon FGH (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The future of health isn't about choosing between pharmaceuticals and supplements – it's about understanding how both can work together. This panel explores the need for science-backed supplementation as food systems evolve and health challenges become more complex. We'll break down the false dichotomy between pharmaceuticals and natural supplements, highlighting how reactive medicine and proactive supplementation can coexist. Through expert insights, we'll bridge pharmaceutical literacy with supplement science, empowering consumers to make informed decisions about integrative nutrition.
