Embodied Regulation in a Dysregulated World

Event ID: PP1150177

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Room 412 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Insight doesn’t change lives. Integration does. Trauma isn’t stored in memory. Leadership isn’t built in theory. Both live in the body — and the nervous system that governs it. This immersive session is a nervous system reset for a world stuck in chronic fight, flight, and freeze — a living portal between ancient medicine and modern neuroscience. The experience begins with a short, grounded presentation on the power of breath and how it directly influences the sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous systems, shaping emotion, decision-making, and presence in real time. Participants are then guided through a trauma-informed somatic breathwork and meditation practice, using mouth breathing to safely release trapped emotional and survival energy — without storytelling or retraumatization. The session transitions into nasal breathing, supporting embodiment, integration, and regulation — moving the body from release into grounded coherence. We close with a live ocean drum ritual, using rhythm and vibration to anchor integration at a cellular level, followed by an optional sharing circle for connection and reflection. This is not self-optimization. This is embodied regulation. Participants leave regulated, present, and connected — with a lived understanding of breath as a tool for emotional regulation, resilience, and conscious leadership.
