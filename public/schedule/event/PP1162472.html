How B2B Creators Are Driving the Next $100M Wave

Event ID: PP1162472

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

While everyone is focused on channels like TikTok and Instagram, B2B creators are quietly building the next $100M wave in the creator economy. These "professional creators" aren’t chasing trends, they’re trusted experts turning LinkedIn, newsletters, and podcasts into engines for influence and revenue. This panel brings together leading voices from both sides of the ecosystem (Brand and Creator) to explore how creators are transforming B2B go-to-market strategies, reshaping brand trust, and unlocking new ways to drive growth in high-consideration industries.
