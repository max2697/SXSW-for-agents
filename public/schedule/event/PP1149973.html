Featured Session: Real-World Impact of AI: Opportunities, Risks and Intellectual Property Oh My!

Event ID: PP1149973

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Salon 6-8 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

AI is the most transformative technology of our generation. This session explores the promise and perils of AI, sharing concrete examples of how AI is being applied with impact by businesses, creators and others to drive operational efficiency, enhance customer experiences, increase revenues and augment creativity. The audience will also learn about the risks associated with AI including some novel intellectual property issues (IP) posed, and how to weigh opportunities with risks. The insights shared are from a speaker who has built AI solutions and been at the top levels of technology, law, business and government and boardroom, all of which perspectives are critical to the successful AI applications.
