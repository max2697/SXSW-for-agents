The Skin–Brain Connection: How the Microbiome Relates to Stress and Mood

Event ID: PP1162481

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Salon C (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Groundbreaking new research reveals for the first time that the skin microbiome – particularly the presence of Cutibacterium – is linked to lower stress and improved mood. In this solo session, Dr Samaras elucidates the link that was uncovered - via the skin–brain axis - and what it means for the future of beauty, mental health, and product innovation. Using advanced AI models, they decoded complex microbiome data to uncover patterns no human could detect. This is not just skincare – it’s a new frontier in holistic wellbeing. And imagine the unlocks when we add the power of quantum computing.
