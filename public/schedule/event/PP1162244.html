The New Lab Partner: AI and Future Scientific Discovery

Event ID: PP1162244

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon D (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

AI is quickly becoming an active thinking partner, completely changing scientific discovery. That means, potentially faster cures, new drug uses, and insights to combat viruses. But it sparks key philosophical questions: Who owns AI-generated insight? How do we validate it? Are we more innovative or dependent? Join experts to explore AI's role in surfacing new questions, defining co-discovery, and navigating its risks/rewards for human health. A critical conversation on AI's radical impact on discovery.
