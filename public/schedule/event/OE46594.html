Your Data Isn't Ready - Your Business Can Be with Agentic AI

Event ID: OE46594

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Summary

Featuring: Jimmy Priestas, CMT AI and Data Lead, Americas, Accenture <p> AI moved past the novelty phase. The leaders winning now are wiring it into how work gets done—end‑to‑end—with humans in the lead and AI agents executing and orchestrating. Join us at SXSW for an inside‑out look at how to move from pilots to enterprise‑scale outcomes: faster time‑to‑insight across decisions, redesigned workflows that release 20–40% capacity, and a data foundation that powers secure, cross‑functional impact. We’ll unpack what actually scales—operating models, skills, and governance—not just tools. You’ll see a live multi‑agent demo tackling a real business process, hear client‑tested patterns to modernize data without rip‑and‑replace, and learn how to balance digital sovereignty with global innovation. Walk away with a practical roadmap you can apply Monday morning: the 90‑day moves, the 12‑month reinvention arcs, and the metrics that belong on your P&L—so AI funds growth instead of just cutting cost. <p>
