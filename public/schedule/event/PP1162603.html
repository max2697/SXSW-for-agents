How the Pop-Tarts Bowl Became the Biggest Name in CFB

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

Summary

In the highly competitive and tradition-rich landscape of college athletics, few brand partnerships have achieved the level of cultural impact and consumer engagement as the Pop-Tarts Bowl. What began as a straightforward sponsorship evolved into a standout case study in modern sports marketing, illustrating how creativity, cultural relevance, and bold brand identity can redefine the value of a naming rights agreement.
