It All Begins With Birds – Insights, Joy, and Lessons from Avian Life

Event ID: PP1148577

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Explore the vital role birds play in our world. Not only do birds contribute to biodiversity and the stability of ecosystems, birdwatching carries psychological and emotional benefits – inspiring creativity, mindfulness, and a sense of wonder. By observing birds, we can learn about adaptation, social interaction, and survival strategies. Whether you are a seasoned birdwatcher or simply enjoy the cheerful songs outside your window, this session promises to deepen your appreciation for birds and their essential place in our lives, encouraging you to observe, protect, and celebrate our feathered friends.
