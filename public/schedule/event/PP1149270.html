Designing the Future of Human Performance

Summary

What happens when elite athleticism meets cutting-edge technology? This session explores how human performance, innovation, and storytelling converge to push the boundaries of what’s possible in sport and beyond. A conversation with Paralympian Hunter Woodhall, Olympic Gold medalist Tara Davis-Woodhall, Deloitte’s US CMO Scott Mager, and Narrative principal Justin Moore-Lewy.
