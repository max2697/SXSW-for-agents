SXSW Episodic Pitch-A-Thon presented by SeriesFest

Event ID: PP1149213

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Bridge Ballroom AB (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Back for its seventh year, the SXSW Episodic Pitch-A-Thon presented by SeriesFest gives filmmakers a chance to take their series ideas to the next level—while audiences get a front-row look at the art of the pitch! Independent filmmakers selected for SXSW Film + Television 2026 will present their projects directly to top executives from television, news media, and digital platforms—on stage, in front of a live audience, and a panel of industry experts. Don’t miss this exclusive opportunity to advance your project—and your career. SXSW 2026 creators: apply now to make your pitch unforgettable!
