Co-Creating the Future of XR Distribution

Event ID: PP1162335

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Venue: Guadalupe (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As immersive media rapidly evolves, we face a critical window of opportunity to shape an equitable distribution ecosystem. Join the Independent XR Distribution Coalition for a participatory workshop on imagining a flourishing XR field. The session will begin with a presentation of distribution pipeline research and a database of venues and platforms, followed by a mapping exercise that surfaces the issues artists, audiences, and producers face in XR. Participants will sketch speculative distribution pathways across diverse platforms, using artist-centered strategies as building blocks.
