More Analog, Less Noise Please: City Builders Meet Up

Event ID: PP1150603

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Brazos (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

More Analog, Less Noise Please is a facilitated meet up for people working across city building, design, culture, and innovation who are interested in rhythm, pause, and recovery in fast systems. Rooted in urban life and public space, the conversation explores how analog experiences, emotional infrastructure, and informal release valves shape connection and resilience. The meet up is designed as a shared conversation that invites reflection, observation, and exchange around how cities and the systems around them can better support human pace.
