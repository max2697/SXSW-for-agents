Summary

Quanna is a Savannah-born, New York–based creative entrepreneur, artist, and cultural organizer, best known as the founder of CareFreeBlackGirl, a nationally recognized lifestyle brand celebrating Black femmes through music, community, and entrepreneurship. As a musician, Quanna has released multiple projects, including Q, Miss Thang, and her 2025 single “Rollin Up”, and plans to release more music in 2026. Her sound blends hip-hop, R&B, and alternative influences, showcasing her versatility as an artist. Over the last year, she has performed at major cultural events including SXSW, Charlotte Pride, Toronto Pride, Brooklyn Fashion Week, and more, consistently using her platform to amplify Black femme voices. At SXSW, Quanna continues to merge music, community advocacy, and cultural programming, creating space for creativity, connection, and visibility for Black women and femmes nationwide
