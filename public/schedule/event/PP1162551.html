Tradwives, Throuples, Trends: What Modern Love Means for Ads

Event ID: PP1162551

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 3 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

We are obsessed with romantic love. But relationship dynamics in America are changing and other forms of love—from platonic to filial to digital—are taking priority in our lives. These loves aren't secondary, they shape our identities and steer our decisions. There's a reckoning that romantic love can't and shouldn't fulfill all your needs. In a culture where gender roles, dating trends, and intimacy are being renegotiated in real time, how should brands respond?
