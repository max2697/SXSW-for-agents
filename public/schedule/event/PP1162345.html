Animation Professionals Meet Up

Event ID: PP1162345

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Whitis (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The Animation Professionals Meet Up is a chance to mix with animation industry folks and make some sweet connections. This event is hosted by SXSW veterans Craig Staggs and Steph Swope of Minnow Mountain, animators behind Tower (PBS), Undone (Amazon), and Apollo 10 1/2 (Netflix). Animation pros at any level are invited to join us for the third year of this epic exchange of business cards and social media profiles. Leave your mark on our huge live-drawing canvas featuring the amazing cutting-edge technology of a Sharpie. These are your people!
