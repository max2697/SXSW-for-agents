Escape The Internet (Part 1)

Event ID: FS19239

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 3:15 AM - 4:05 AM

Venue: Alamo Lamar 8 (Alamo Lamar)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The internet is in shambles... and it's time to leave it for good. In this game built for a movie theater, you and the audience play as internet refugees armed only with your phones. Your mission? To escape the AI slop apocalypse and reach the Island of Sanctuary, rumoured to be the last good place on the Internet. From legendary creator Lucas Rizzotto, "Escape The Internet" blends cinema, gaming and theater to create an entirely new genre of live entertainment - one that will make you laugh, cry, and reveal who you - and the ones around you - truly are.
