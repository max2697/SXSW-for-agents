You Are the Visionary: How to Stay Authentic While Using AI

Event ID: PP1162340

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Venue: Guadalupe (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

If an artwork is to be ours, it must be a product of our own vision. AI assistants are readily available. But how do we make sure we never give away so much to a virtual assistant that nothing we create feels like it is authentically ours anymore? How can we keep the joy, the purpose, and the power of our making while engaging AI to support the project of our work coming into being? This workshop will offer a series of core principles, plus meditation and journaling practices, designed to help participants intuit which parts of their project to do themselves and which to outsource.
