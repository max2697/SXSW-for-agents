The New Arena: How Sports, Fans & Culture Are Evolving

Event ID: PP1148570

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Sports fandom is evolving from passive viewership to active, always-on participation. Fans, Athletes, and Creators are reshaping how culture is made, shared, and celebrated. This session explores how brands can authentically tap into the new dynamics of fandom, from game days to off-seasons. Learn how Athletes are the new storytellers, how fan communities are driving cultural moments, and how marketers can show up with relevance and purpose in this new era of sports and engagement.
