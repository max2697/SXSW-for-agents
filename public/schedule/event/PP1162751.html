Come Play at Grief Camp: Healing with Story & Community

Event ID: PP1162751

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Room 412 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

What if you could spend an hour at SXSW… at grief camp? A place where everyone’s been through something hard, and no one pretends they’re “fine.” Experience Camps is flipping the script on how we process grief through play, connection, and clinically informed practices. In this immersive workshop. clinically-trained counselors and facilitators will guide you through activities designed to explore the expression and processing of grief. It’s playful, messy, cathartic, and it’s for anyone ready to rethink grief and connection. Grievers and non-grievers are welcome!
