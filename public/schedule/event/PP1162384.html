Dissolve Lab: Clear Your Invisible Barrier Without Burnout

Event ID: PP1162384

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Venue: Rio Grande Ballroom 2 (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

This immersive, hands-on workshop guides participants through The Dissolve: a nervous-system-safe method for moving through invisible barriers without force or burnout. You’ll name your own stuck point, map its emotional signature, and use tools like breathwork, the Feelings Wheel, and guided visualization to shift it. Walk away with a customized clarity map and a gentle, resonant next step—designed to move you forward with ease instead of push.
