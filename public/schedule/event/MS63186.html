REHMA

Event ID: MS63186

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 1:55 AM - 2:25 AM

Venue: Flamingo Cantina

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Inspired by her culturally rich & diverse upbringing, between her Pakistani-Muslim heritage to her formative days in Chicago, REHMA's sonic worlds are eclectic & vibrant. The singer-songwriter artfully weaves her powerful vocals with poetic lyricism, floating between pop, dance, and R&B, to create a singular soundscape & aesthetic that she coins as "desi-futurism" Off the back a buzzing 2024, with a massive feature on Coke Studio (100M+ views across platforms), the cover of EQUAL Pakistan & a top slot on the Best Of Fresh Finds Year-End List), & earned co-signs from Savan Kotecha, Natasha Noorani, Zulfi, Sid Sriram & more. She's cemented herself as a promising creative force, not only in the rising South Asian diaspora, but across global pop music. Her forthcoming project, ANOMALY, is a refreshing take on modern pop & a timely, urgent coming of age story from the Pakistani-Muslim multi-hyphenate as she navigates her identity, religion, sexuality, and femininity.
