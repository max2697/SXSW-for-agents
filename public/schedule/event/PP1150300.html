Summary

Startup founders face relentless pressure, emotional volatility, and decision fatigue. This session explores how intentional psychedelic experiences, combined with integration practices, can expand perspective, dissolve ego-driven blind spots, and strengthen leadership presence. Drawing from The Mind Shift Network’s work with executives and entrepreneurs, we’ll examine how psychedelics catalyze authentic innovation, emotional intelligence, and purpose-driven company culture.
