Summary

Canon is an independent artist who turns pain into purpose and lyrics into lifelines. Raised between Chicago and Atlanta, his music is raw, cinematic, and rooted in real-life battles—surviving trauma, questioning faith, rebuilding identity, and refusing to quit. Every bar is personal, every beat intentional. He doesn’t make music to impress—he makes it to connect, to challenge, to heal. Fully hands-on with his sound, visuals, and message, Canon creates from a place of truth, and invites his listeners to face theirs. This isn’t just music—it’s movement. Welcome to the journey.
