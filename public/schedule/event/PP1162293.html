Beyond the Grid: Motorsport Culture Meet Up

Event ID: PP1162293

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Brazos (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Motorsport is having a cultural breakthrough from mainstream moments like Drive to Survive and the F1 Movie to sim racing, TikTok fandoms, and grassroots club culture. However, most spaces still center the same legacy voices. This Meetup brings together fans, creators, marketers, and community builders who are shaping what motorsport looks like next. Whether you're growing a following, building a brand, or simply finding your people, this is a space to connect, share stories, and spotlight rising voices across all motorsport passion points.
