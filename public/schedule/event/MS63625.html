Summary

Leo Von Sol is a pop and R&B artist based in Austin, Texas, pairing sleek pop songwriting with R&B phrasing, and a lower baritone vocal presence. Influenced by artists like Tinashe, KAI of EXO, and Omar Apollo, his work blends polished performance instincts with a cinematic visual sensibility and modern production choices. His recent EP, Garden of Sol, is focused on themes of nostalgia, temptation, and solitude, expressed through bright pop and R&B sonics and sun-rich visuals that draw from early-2000s pop language without feeling like a throwback. A Recording Academy member, Leo Von Sol balances candid, behind-the-scenes authenticity with a professionally branded presence, while his style sits at the intersection of luxury streetwear and high fashion, always intentional, elevated, and lived-in.
