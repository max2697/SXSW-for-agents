Summary

Sonora Tukukuy is a Miami-based music collective, known for its modern take on roots cumbia rhythms blended with modern sounds fueling Miami’s diverse community and its permanent growth. Sonora Tukukuy’s fresh approach, combined with the energetic, danceable nature of cumbia,has helped the genre gain traction at local venues, festivals, and clubs. They’ve contributed to the resurgence of Latin American folk music in modern spaces, showing how it can be reinterpreted and made relevant in a cosmopolitan environment. Their proposal is enriched by the diverse backgrounds of its members, who come from various Latin American countries and beyond. This multicultural mix brings different musical influences, perspectives, and styles into their work, resulting in a fusion of sounds that appeals to a broad range of audiences. The band's ability to incorporate various genres and rhythms while maintaining the core essence of cumbia demonstrates how traditional music can evolve and connect with contemporary global cultures.
