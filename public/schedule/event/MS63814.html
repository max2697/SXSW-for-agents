Grace Sorensen

Event ID: MS63814

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 12:00 AM - 12:30 AM

Venue: Zilker Brewing

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Grace Sorensen is one of the fastest independent-rising stars in the music scene. As a multi-hyphenate singer, songwriter, and dancer, she’s been dubbed "Austin's Wonderkid" and “Austin’s R&B Cool Girl” by the Austin Chronicle and KUTX. In her young career she has already been featured on Joe Budden’s “Sleeper Picks,” collaborated with BLK ODYSSY, been inducted to the Recording Academy's Class of 2025, played Austin City Limits Festival, SXSW Austin & London, and surpassed one million organic streams on her single "Digits.” She debuted her versatile album "BLUEPRINT" February 2025 and has exciting expectation of new collaborations, visual projects, and records.
