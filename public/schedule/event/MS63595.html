Summary

Batry Powr is Hundred Waters frontwoman Nicole Miglis exploring long-form meditation music with battery-powered instruments. Nicole Miglis is a songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and producer who's music has been featured in publications including Pitchfork, Stereogum, Rolling Stone, NPR and The New York Times. Her band Hundred Waters have performed on the David Letterman and Stephen Colbert late night shows, as well as festivals including Coachella, Bonnaroo, and the band’s own festival FORM. From 2022-24 she toured as the lead vocalist for Bonobo’s Fragments world tour, and artist with whom she’s collaborated with, most recently for the 2025 soundtrack of Shinichirō Watanabe’s latest anime series, Lazarus. Her live performances feature her love of blending electronic and acoustic instrumentation, and background in classical piano, into a musical form form all her own.
