Make it in Texas! Film, TV and Digital Media Meet Up

Event ID: PP1162358

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Whitis (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Love to make movies, shows or digital media content? Do you have a project to produce in Texas? Are you wanting to connect with the Texas production community and beyond? If so, the Texas Film Commission invites you to come chat about ways to make it happen. Let’s talk about locations, local production crews, vendors, grants, incentives, new technologies and the creative community in the Lone Star State. We want to share with you the many resources that Texas has to offer as a destination for your next project.
