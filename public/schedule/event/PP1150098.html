Space Workforce Meet Up

Event ID: PP1150098

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Brazos (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Texas has always been the frontier of America’s biggest ideas - from cattle drives to microchips, music revolutions to moonshots. In 2026, the frontier is shifting upward, and the call for the New Space Cowboy is here. In this era of commercial space, the next giant leap won’t be driven by astronauts, but welders, coders, machinists, technicians, scientists, mission operators, and dreamers. And no place on Earth is better positioned to cultivate this next gen of talent. Join the Space Workforce Incubator for Texas for a meetup that blends the spirit of the Wild West with the future's promise.
