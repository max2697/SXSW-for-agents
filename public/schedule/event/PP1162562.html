Behind The Viral Hit: Why It's Not What It Seems

Event ID: PP1162562

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Capitol Ballroom FGH (Downright Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

In an era where TikTok virality can catapult unknown artists to millions of streams overnight, the music industry faces a critical question: how do we transform fleeting moments into lasting careers? This panel brings together industry experts to lift the lid on viral streams and explore strategic frameworks that separate one-hit wonders from career artists. Drawing from real-world case studies, the discussion will provide actionable insights for artists, managers, and industry professionals looking to build careers that transcend trending sounds and create lasting cultural impact.
