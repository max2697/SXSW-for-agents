Summary

Bohemio is a collective of friends from different cities, united by music and a shared creative vision. Blending their talent with natural charisma, they’ve taken off on social media, inviting audiences to connect with their youthful energy and rich musical roots. Positioned at the forefront of the new wave of cumbia and regional Mexican music, Bohemio fuses the genre’s tradition with a fresh, original sound. They’ve built a highly engaged fanbase on TikTok and Instagram, where their modern cumbias frequently go viral and become part of their audience’s everyday soundtrack, setting them apart as one of the most promising acts in today’s regional scene.
