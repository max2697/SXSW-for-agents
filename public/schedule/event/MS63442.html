60 JUNO

Event ID: MS63442

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 1:50 AM - 2:30 AM

Venue: Hotel Vegas

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Emerging from Merced, CA, 60 JUNO (sixty juno) leads the Central Valley Post-Punk scene with a blend of his own creation as he merges DIY Indie-Rock with Post-Punk elements to create a notable sound worth remembering. By incorporating heightened energy and rough, low vocals into the usual wistful, dreamy componenets typical of the Indie-Rock genre, 60 JUNO has truly created a lane of his own making. The independent 22-year old musician is the sole driving force behind the bass, drums, guitar and vocals that seamlessly translate heavy nostalgia into deep, dreamy melodies.
