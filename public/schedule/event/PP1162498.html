Summary

Join us for an engaging fireside chat featuring Influential Chief Communications Officer, Chris Detert, in conversation with one of today’s most recognizable digital personalities, Jordan “The Stallion” Howlett. Known for his infectious energy, unfiltered honesty, and viral storytelling across platforms like TikTok, Jordan has become a leading voice in the creator economy. From his signature food reviews to his candid takes on everyday life, he’s built a devoted community by staying true to himself—while navigating the business of brand deals, audience engagement, and online growth.
