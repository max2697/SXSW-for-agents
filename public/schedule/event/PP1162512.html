Data Equity Core Conversation

Event ID: PP1162512

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Room 410 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As data becomes the raw material of modern life, how do we ensure it's collected, shared, and used in ways that build, not break, trust? This structured meet-up invites technologists , policymakers, and community advocates to explore responsible data practices across industries. Together, we’ll reflect on the disappearance of public data, rising mistrust in institutions, and what it means to build for integrity, transparency, and equity. Participants will share challenges and insights, contribute to a collective best practices, and connect with others shaping the future of ethical data use.
