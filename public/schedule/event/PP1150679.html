For the Students Meet Up

Event ID: PP1150679

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM

Venue: Music Clubhouse (Downright Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Following How to Get the Job: Building a Career in Music, FTS will open doors for a casual mixer and industry networking session. FTS team members will be on the ground alongside panelists Sofia Martinez (WME) and Philip Payne (Plan A Consulting). With Philip serving as the professor behind FTS' Music Business Foundations program and Sofia being an alumni of the program herself, this will be a good-spirited community meet-up where badge-holders from across the music industry can connect, swap stories, and chop it up about their individual pathways into the space.
