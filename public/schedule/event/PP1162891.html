The New Cool: Choosing Moderation in a World of Excess

Event ID: PP1162891

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

New generations are redefining consumption, choosing balance, moderation, meaning, and authenticity over excess. Using Georgetown University’s case study of AB InBev’s Global Smart Drinking Goals, this session explores how brands can leverage marketing expertise and innovations that meet consumers’ desire for more balanced choices, such as lower and no-alcohol beer, and influence to shape social norms and create shared value. The discussion highlights how purpose-driven strategies can drive behavior change, reinforce positive behaviors, promote moderation, and deliver sustainable growth.
