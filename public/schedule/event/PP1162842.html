The Burnout Economy: Leading in an Age of Exhaustion

Event ID: PP1162842

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon B (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Exhaustion is no longer just a personal issue—it’s now an economic concern. Leaders across industries are burning out, leading to short-term thinking and diminished organizational resilience. Dr Daniel Motta will draw on research in economics, organizational behavior, and leadership to show how exhausted executives impact everything from market performance to employee engagement and introduces new frameworks for fostering sustainable leadership that safeguards both people and performance.
