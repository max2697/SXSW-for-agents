Featured Session: Craft Still Wins: The Irreplaceable Role of Human Instinct, Taste, and Emotion

Event ID: PP1148516

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Hilton Grand Ballroom (Salon HJK) (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Have we lost sight that the creative work that truly impacts you on a human level, requires just that—the craft of a human? Greg Greenberg, award-winning Executive Creative Director at TBWA\Media Arts Lab, Apple’s global creative agency explores the irreplaceable role of human instinct, taste, and emotional intelligence in the creative process. Featuring real-world examples of handcrafted storytelling, he offers a rare perspective on how we can’t lose sight of what truly impacts people on a deep emotional level, human craft, even in a world infatuated by the speed, ease and impact of AI.
