Pitch Camp! How To Win Creative Pitches by Being More Human

Event ID: PP1162220

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Venue: Brazos I-III (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Let's be honest, pitching clients for new business is a slog.  Whether you're seeking new partners or locked in a 5-way competition, there is a way to cut through the madness and remain calm. This workshop will focus on human connection, now more important than ever in the age of AI. We will share hands-on tools for deepening client relationships, getting the right flow in your pitch, collaborating with your team, and standing out in a world of sameness. The facilitators have years of experience on both sides of the pitch table: at Frog, McKinsey, Philips, and Publicis Sapient.
