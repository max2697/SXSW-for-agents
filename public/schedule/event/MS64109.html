Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 3:00 AM - 3:20 AM

Summary

As was foretold by the prophecy, the SpaceGoonz have entered this plane of existence. They crash-landed in Austin, Texas, in 2023. Their current mission: to reinvent what it means to be a Hip-Hop artist in the capital city of Texas. They are returning to SXSW for the third consecutive year, continuing to help elevate the local Hip-Hop scene into a nationally recognized hub for the art form. Comparable to Wu-Tang Clan, Brockhampton, Flatbush Zombies, ETC. Consisting of members from all around the country and spanning multiple generations, their sound is a collage of the different Hip-Hop practices, melded together by the diverse backgrounds of every member. So it doesn’t matter what god you believe in or what media you consume, as long as, when we say “Space,” you say… “Goonz”
