Two-Man Giant Squid

Event ID: MS63883

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 10:00 PM - 10:40 PM

Venue: The 13th Floor

View on Official SXSW

Summary

A post-punk project out of Brooklyn NY, Two-Man Giant Squid has earned acclaim in the New York City underground for their ability to fuse dance-punk, garage, and psych influences into fierce live shows and ambitious recordings (KEXP, SPIN). They lean on an eclectic mix to produce their sound including matter-of-fact vocal delivery, intricate synth design, and lately- a theremin. Their most recent LP was unveiled at last year’s SXSW festival and later charted at #89 on National Indie Radio charts. With a slate of releases & tours planned for 2026, Two-Man Giant Squid brings their signature creativity and DIY spirit to the forefront of the post-punk revival.
