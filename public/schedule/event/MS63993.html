Astrokeyy

Event ID: MS63993

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 1:00 AM - 1:10 AM

Venue: Flamingo Cantina

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Astrokeyy is a Houston-based hip-hop artist carving his own lane with sharp lyricism, atmospheric production, and a sound rooted in Southern culture with a forward-thinking edge. Known for his commanding live presence, Astrokeyy has been selected as an **official SXSW artist twice**, cementing his reputation as a standout performer on one of the world’s most influential stages. He has shared stages with respected names in hip-hop including **Rome Streetz, Jay Worthy, and Devin The Dude**, holding his own alongside both underground heavyweights and Southern legends. Recently, Astrokeyy released his latest project "Southern Hospitality" fully produced by Marc Spano and executive produced by Dough Networkz a cohesive body of work that highlights his growth, Southern identity, and refined sonic vision. Blending raw storytelling, confident delivery, and hypnotic beats, Astrokeyy represents the grind, ambition, and creativity of Houston’s new generation. With momentum fueled by high-profile performances and a focused artistic direction, he continues pushing his sound forward with longevity, authenticity, and impact in mind.
