Beyond the Sample: The 3D Fashion Revolution You Don’t See

Event ID: PP1162527

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom A (Austin Marriott Downtown)

Summary

From eliminating physical samples to populating product pages and TikTok ads with real-time renders, 3D is quietly reshaping the entire fashion lifecycle. This panel dives into what happens behind the scenes—from factory floor to homepage—with leaders across design, supply chain, and digital content discussing the hidden efficiencies, sustainable breakthroughs, and creative freedoms unlocked by 3D technology.
