Summary

Tia Tutt, born on January 6th in Augusta, GA, hails from Lincolnton, GA. From an early age, she faced the profound loss of her brother, shaping her journey with profound challenges. However, Tia found solace and expression in music, which became her refuge and passion. Her path led her to Los Angeles, where she endured a grueling two-year period of homelessness. Despite the adversity, Tia persevered, channeling her experiences into her music. During this time, she recorded an impressive four mixtapes, each containing 20 songs, showcasing her resilience and dedication to her craft. Despite the hardships she faced, Tia emerged stronger, using her artistry as a means of empowerment and self-expression. Today, she continues to live vibrantly, thriving as both a person and an artist, constantly evolving and creating music that resonates with audiences around the world. Tia’s journey is a testament to the power of perseverance and the transformative nature of music in the face of adversity.
