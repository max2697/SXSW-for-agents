Summary

Elijah Delgado is a 23-year-old singer-songwriter from Austin, Texas, creating what he calls Indie Rock Lullabies — a blend of cinematic energy, textured guitar work, and raw emotion. Since releasing his debut EP When I Was On Cloud 9 in 2021, which surpassed 700,000 Spotify streams, Elijah has emerged as one of Austin’s most compelling young voices. He’s performed on some of the city’s biggest stages, including Blues On The Green 2025 for 20,000+ fans, the national anthem at Austin FC, and opening slots for Chaparelle, Next of Kin, Zach Person, and West 22nd. Festival highlights include Old Settler’s, UtopiaFest, Hot Summer Nights, and ACL Radio’s Drop-In, alongside headlining milestones like Meridian’s first sold-out show and a packed Stubb’s Indoors set following Dayglow. His latest release, Moonboy (June 2025), showcases a bold evolution. The explosive single “Hide + Seek” contrasts with the aching title track, "Moonboy", exploring isolation, hope, and transformation. With vulnerable lyrics, dynamic shifts, and soulful delivery, Elijah is building a loyal audience and cementing his place as a rising Austin artist to watch.
