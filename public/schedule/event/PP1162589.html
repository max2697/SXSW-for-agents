Brands x Music: Bringing together Activations & Artists

Event ID: PP1162589

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 4-6 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Some brands don’t just show up - they create cultural moments. Today’s most innovative marketers are blending bold brand activations with the power of music and star power to build emotional resonance and spark global buzz. In this lively discussion, hear from marketing leaders at the world’s most forward-thinking brands as they explore how to craft unforgettable experiences that connect with consumers on a visceral level, through sound, story, and shared energy. Learn how music-fueled campaigns are driving loyalty, elevating brand perception, and shaping the future of experiential marketing.
