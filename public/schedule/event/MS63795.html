DJ Trendsetter Sense + Trendsetter Cypher

Event ID: MS63795

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 12:00 AM - 2:00 AM

Venue: Brushy Street Commons

View on Official SXSW

Summary

DJ Sense, a founding member of "The Aphilliates, " gained recognition as Trendsetter Sense. Co Creator of the famous brand “Gangsta Grillz & Gangsta Grillz Radio, landing shows on Eminem's Shade 45 and Atlanta's Hot 107.9, reaching over 30 million listeners. He then produced the mixtape series"Rhythm & Streets”,collaborating with artists like Monica, Chris Brown, T Pain, Akon, Usher, K.Michelle & more. In 2016, he partnered with Lil Jon, managing his worldwide tour. Trendsetter expands his influence with Chosen Journey, a new chapter in his brand that merges Faith, Music and Culture. Through this platform, he uplifts artist perspectives in modern media while embracing a transformative path rooted in faith based and purpose driven living. Chosen Journey made history with the first ever Christian Hip Hop set at Rolling Loud Miami 2024. Returning with CHOSEN JOURNEY PART II ROLLING LOUD ORLANDO 2026. The Rise of Christian Hip Hop has opened a powerful new door that a new generation desires to be filled with a message of fullfillment and inspiration. Catch Trendsetter Sense’s Chosen Journey shows every Sunday from 8- 10am ET on HOT 107.9 ATL and SiriusXM Hip Hop Nation 10-12p ET.
