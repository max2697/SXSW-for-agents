Syncrets to Success: Conversation With Music Supervisors

Event ID: PP1162267

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Capitol Ballroom FGH (Downright Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Want to get your music placed in TV, film, or games? Hear directly from top music supervisors on how they discover music, what makes a great pitch, and why some tracks get skipped. We’ll break down how artists can build trust, when to reach out, and how sync agents help. This is a candid conversation for music creators serious about sync and a continuation from the SXSW experiences I brought in 2025 — with real advice from the people who make the placements happen.
