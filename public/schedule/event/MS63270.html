Summary

"A voice that stops you in your tracks" – BBC Radio 1 Hailing from Leicester, UK, Morgan Munroe is one of the most exciting voices in contemporary R&B. Fresh off four standout sets at Glastonbury 2025 and recent performances at SXSW London and SXSW Texas, this award-winning powerhouse continues to push boundaries. She’s earned a BBC Radio 1 Track of the Week, featured on Ghetts’ latest album, and received recognition from Vogue Italia, Mahalia, and DJ Ace. With over 33K monthly listeners on Spotify, her music has landed on the FIFA 2020 soundtrack and gained support from some of the industry's most respected tastemakers. As part of the MOBO Unsung Class of 2025, Morgan Munroe is showing the world exactly why she’s one to watch - on stage, on record, and beyond.
