Summary

Pierce Washington is an award-winning hip-hop artist from Dallas, Texas, whose sound bridges jazz influences, live instrumentation, and modern production — creating music that feels both timeless and forward-thinking. Known for his cinematic storytelling and commanding stage presence, Pierce has emerged as one of the leading voices in Dallas hip-hop. His collaborations with legendary producer Madlib and rap icon Lil Wayne showcase his versatility and creative range. Fresh off headlining The Proof of Concept and Folktales tours, Pierce continues to push artistic boundaries through his innovative approach to music, visuals, and brand artistry.
