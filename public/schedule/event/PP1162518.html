User Centric Design & Funding of Accessible Tech

Event ID: PP1162518

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Salon E (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

This panel will focus on user centered design (UCD) as the crux of successful product development - with inclusion at the core. Founders with disabilities will discuss their experiences developing technology and services - focusing on how they undertook UCD, identified testers in their communities, and ultimately - funded those initiatives and brought them to market.
