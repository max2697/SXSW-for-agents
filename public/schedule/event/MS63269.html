Summary

Primed to be the next group from the UK jazz underground to break out, Azamiah draw on jazz roots while touching on everything from James Blake’s production techniques to dubby low-end, taking the listener on a journey across moody and light landscapes. Their 2023 debut album ‘In Phases’ won praise from The Skinny and CLASH and received plays on BBC 6Music and NTS. A bridge between first album, and second, their recent EP ‘Two Lands’ released in May 2025 resonates with emotional honesty and is played with a fresh intensity. The band has played on stages across the UK and Europe including Latitude Festival, Jazz on Wick, Hidden Door, Edinburgh Jazz and Blues Festival, Brick Lane Jazz Festival, JAZZMI (IT) & North Sea Round Town (NL). Having recently completed their first UK headline tour in October 2025 to electric audiences in Glasgow, London, Bristol, Manchester, Leeds and Brighton and flooded with requests for a re-press of 'In Phases', a dedicated fanbase is making themselves known.
