Summary

Lovpune is a queer indie-electronic pop artist who moved from conservative Texas to Los Angeles. She channels that transformation into late-night soundscapes perfect for empty highways and midnight dance floor connections. Writing and producing her own music, she creates infectious hooks wrapped in shimmering melodies. Lovpune is a soundtrack to stories of chosen family and authentic self-discovery.
