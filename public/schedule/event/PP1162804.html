Shortchanged: Gen Z, the Economy, and Brands

Event ID: PP1162804

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As economic opportunity slips further out of reach for young adults, brands face a new reality: a generation facing high living costs, stagnant wages, and declining opportunity is redefining how, why, and where they spend. This session explores how financial precarity is reshaping consumer behavior and what it means for marketers. Learn how to build relevance and win over a generation that’s not just broke, but breaking the mold.
