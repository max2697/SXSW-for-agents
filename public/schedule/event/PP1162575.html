Two Futurists and an AI: Seeing Trouble Ahead

Event ID: PP1162575

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon AB (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join celebrity futurist Faith Popcorn, techno-futurist Sarah DaVanzo, and Delph.Ai, an AI Synthetic Human trained on the minds of female futurists in a boundary-pushing, three-way debate on the future of leadership in the age of intelligent machines. With Faith’s dystopian view, Sarah’s utopian counterpoint, and Delph.Ai’s unexpected input, this session explores the trouble coming as AI challenges the very definition of human agency, influence, and power.
