Summary

Ruido Selecto is a musician, producer, and sound engineer from Medellín, Colombia. His music focuses on Caribbean rhythms such as Cumbia, Salsa, Electronic Dub, and Afro-diasporic styles that have traditionally reached Colombia through the Picó Soundsystem culture. Ruido defines his sound as "Caribbean Bass." Since 2015, he has released two studio albums and several collaborations and EPs (Secret Lab 2015, Piratería Caribeña y Contrabando 2019 Equador 2022, and Cumbia Soundsystem EP 2025). In 2024, he released a series of remixes of iconic tracks from the legendary Colombian label Discos Fuentes, titled 14 CAÑONAZOS EN DUB, an audiovisual production that was highlighted by the specialized media outlet Gladys Palmera. He has toured nationally and internationally in Mexico and Central America, with additional performances in the United States and Europe. Ruido Selecto has participated in festivals such as Altavoz, África Futura, and Invazion Fest, sharing the stage with artists like Mitú, Afrotronix, Ghetto Kumbé, Systema Solar, Alligatorz, among others. He has worked as a producer and mastering engineer for Discos Fuentes , Folcore Barcelona and No Sabes.
