Will Podcasts Survive the YouTube Era?

Event ID: PP1162784

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom F (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As YouTube becomes the fastest-growing podcast platform and creators scramble to add video to their podcasts, a critical question emerges: Is pure audio dead? In this head-to-head debate, industry leaders will argue whether podcasting must evolve into a visual medium to survive, or if audio's unique intimacy and convenience will always have a place in our media landscape. We'll explore how creators and networks are navigating this visual-first world—from adding cameras to intimate conversations to leveraging video clips for social discovery while preserving audio's storytelling magic.
