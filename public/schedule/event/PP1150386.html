Speaking for the Dead: How True Crime Shapes Justice

Event ID: PP1150386

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

True crime is one of the most powerful ways the public grapples with justice, violence, and accountability. This conversation brings together Thien Ho, Sacramento DA and lead prosecutor on the Golden State Killer case, and historian Anne Soon Choi, whose biography of Thomas Noguchi explores the Coroner to the Stars' most famous cases, including Marilyn Monroe and RFK. The authors explore how law, forensic practice, emerging technology, and storytelling shape how victims are represented, cold cases are solved, and truth is constructed in the public eye. Moderated by publisher Stephanie Lim.
