Workforce Innovation in Production & the Creative Economy

Event ID: PP1162928

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Room 405 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

How can we reimagine training and workforce development in film, media, and the creative industries as technology, production models, and industry needs rapidly evolve? This meetup convenes educators, workforce leaders, and entertainment professionals to explore innovative approaches for preparing set-ready talent and upskilling current professionals. Participants will share strategies, examine gaps, and discuss how policy levers like film tax credits, emerging technology, and cross-sector collaboration can create scalable, equitable pathways from education to sustainable careers.
