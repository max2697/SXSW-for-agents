“It’s in Development” Creating Great TV for Global Platforms

Event ID: PP1162428

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Bridge Ballroom AB (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

From assessing pitches to staffing writers’ rooms to offering script notes and more, Universal Studio Group’s television executives dive-deep into their development processes and share what makes for a strong, mutually advantageous relationship between creators and a studio. Gain insights from the people behind recent & upcoming series, including The Five-Star Weekend, Ponies, We Were Liars, Cape Fear, Too Much, All Her Fault and many more.
