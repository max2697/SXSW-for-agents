Daniel Fears

Event ID: MS63669

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 3:30 AM - 4:10 AM

Venue: The Pershing

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist Daniel Fears knows that music is more than just sound. It’s a journey, capable of taking us to places we’ve never been or bringing us closer to who we really are. With every note, every chord, every lyric, Daniel strives to create something that feels authentic and grounded. Since his debut in 2020, he’s been nominated for “Best R&B” at the Austin Music Awards, opened for internationally-touring artists like Mac Ayres and BLK ODYSSY, had his music featured in an NAACP Image Award-nominated short film, and toured with Latin Grammy Nominee Gina Chavez for the American Music Abroad program. His latest release, a fully-orchestrated live EP “Close To Home”, is available on all streaming services.
