More Pavlov, Less Badges: Gamification Isn't What You Think

Event ID: PP1162304

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Venue: Brazos I-III (Courtyard Marriott)

Summary

Before there were prize wheels, badges, and leaderboards, there was a scientist and his dogs. Join gamification experts Adam Messing and Kinley Clifford to talk about the common misconceptions around gamification and how understanding behavioral and motivational science is the key to unlocking it. This interactive workshop will be jam-packed with fun and useful exercises and games.
