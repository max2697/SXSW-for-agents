Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 8:00 PM - 8:40 PM

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Raised in a sunny southern California beach town, Saige Davis writes country-leaning songs straight from the heart with melodies that feel everlasting. Writing primarily on her own, she creates music that feels grounded and familiar - and yet wholly her own. Influenced by both the country classics and also Americana with a modern edge, her songs lean on acoustic guitars, simple arrangements, and lyrics that leave room to breathe. Whether written quietly at home or brought to life in the studio with producers Caleb Nelson (Mt. Joy) and Patrick Lyons (Colter Wall, Luke Grimes, Noeline Hoffman), Saige’s music shows a deep respect for timeless storytelling and feels imminent for a breakthrough in the year ahead.
