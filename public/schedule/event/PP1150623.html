Modern Makers Meet Up

Event ID: PP1150623

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Making is evolving from traditional craft to digital storytelling. In the era of social media, we’re finding more creators showcase their physical talents online. This Meet Up brings together modern makers working with diverse materials, platforms, and disciplines who are redefining what it means to create today. Whether you’re building by hand, sharing your process online, or blending the physical and digital, this is a space to connect, exchange ideas, and find others shaping the future of making.
