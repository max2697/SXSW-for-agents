Summary

Just Jim is a multidisciplinary creative living in Austin, Texas. Known locally as a club DJ, he has carved out a reputation as a tastemaker behind the decks. He has an instinct for connecting with the crowd, and is beloved for his ability to communicate with music. His sets are as genre-defying as they are expansive, which provides the source code for his unique style of production. Although labeled as "house music," elements of Hip Hop, R&B, jazz, experimental, and world music are seamlessly incorporated to make for a rich sound full of hidden gems.
