Featured Session: Shit You Should Care About: Live

Event ID: PP1149975

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon 6-8 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Ever wondered what it’s like to accidentally start a global media empire from your uni group chat? In this live SXSW show, Lucy Blakiston scrolls back through the feed that is the Shit You Should Care About story. Expect internet reckonings, hot takes, a fangirl-to-SHE-EO pipeline, and behind-the-scenes SYSCA drama - all stitched together with honesty, humour, and a little bit of heartache. It’s like flicking through your favourite Instagram account, but in real life. No bullshit - just Lucy, the posts that changed everything, and what it’s like being a very normal girl trying to stay real in front of a very not-normal number of people.
