Summary

Blair Davie is a singer-songwriter from Perth, Scotland. Inspired by their love of emotional, heartfelt music from artists such as Dodie, Dermot Kennedy and Bon Iver, Blair’s music is their way of presenting the most honest and passionate version of themself to the world. Their music deals with coming to terms with their own bisexuality, loss and heartbreak; but also, the beauty that life can offer.
