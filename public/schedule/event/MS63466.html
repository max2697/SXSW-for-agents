Honey Made

Event ID: MS63466

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 12:00 AM - 12:30 AM

Venue: Saxon Pub

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Austin’s Honey Made delivers a modern, high-energy spin on vintage soul -and funk, channeling legends like James Brown, Sly and the Family Stone, and Earth, Wind & Fire while staying rooted in rich R&B traditions. Honey Made calls its take on contemporary RnB, Texas RnB. It is RnB with Texas Heat. Named Best Live Band in Austin by Austin Fit Magazine twice in a row, the band’s dynamic performances have earned them multiple official SXSW showcases, and stages alongside Karl Denson’s Tiny Universe, The Soul Rebels, Funk You, and The Spin Doctors. With acclaimed releases including Brand New, Charge It To The Band Fund, and Pass Me By, Honey Made is quickly expanding their reach beyond Austin with their infectious hooks, fearless funk grooves, and a sound that invites every listener into the fold.
