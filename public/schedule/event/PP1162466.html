Leading with Both Sides: The Business-Creative Hyphenate

Event ID: PP1162466

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Salon B (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

More professionals than ever are combining business acumen with creative ambition, choosing careers that embrace both. This conversation explores what it takes to lead in corporate spaces and pursue creative passions with purpose. Hear from leaders who’ve scaled brands, launched movements, and redefined success—without “starting over.” We’ll share practical tactics for integrating creative identity into leadership and professional branding, and explore how corporate-to-creative leaders are reshaping success, innovation, and the future of work.
