From Insight to Impact: Superintendents Share What Works

Event ID: PP1162818

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Room 403-404 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Want to understand how superintendents make purchasing decisions? Join leaders from the Institute for Education Innovation (IEI), a national superintendent think tank committed to fostering meaningful partnerships between districts and ed tech innovators. IEI superintendents will share candid insights and firsthand experiences to help founders and executives design solutions that truly support student success. The conversation will be guided by a superintendent emeritus and a 20-year industry veteran who has helped hundreds of ed tech companies grow and scale effectively.
