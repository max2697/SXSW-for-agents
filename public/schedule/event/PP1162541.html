Event ID: PP1162541

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Our digital world risks leaving groups behind. This panel issues a call-to-arms for truly inclusive design in physical spaces, where technology amplifies human potential. With 20% of the world's population identifying as neurodistinct in some way, we'll unveil a blueprint for environments that care for all human needs. Explore how Google and Hypersolid are developing adaptable guidelines to unlock unique cognitive strengths - fostering engagement, productivity, and belonging. Join a revolution of the human mind where every mind shines.
