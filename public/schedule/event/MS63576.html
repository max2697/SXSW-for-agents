Rosa Anschuetz

Event ID: MS63576

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 9:15 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Speakeasy

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Rosa Anschütz is a musician and sound artist whose practice merges composition, installation, and performance. Her sound blends the dark ambience of post-punk and cold wave with ethereal polyphony and synth-driven melodies. She has released three LPs: Votive (2020), accompanied by nine clay sculptures inspired by votive offerings; Goldener Strom (2022), which was staged at the Deutsche Oper Berlin; and Interior (2024), featuring nine tracks, each accompanied by a sequined landscape hand-stitched by Anschütz. Her new album, Sabbatical, was released on September 26, 2025, on Heartworm Press, US.
