Summary

Kaash Paige (born D’Kyla Paige Woolen on January 8, 2001, in Dallas, Texas) is a singer and songwriter known for her smooth blend of R&B, EDM , Alternative, Pop and hip-hop. She began uploading songs to SoundCloud while in high school, breaking out with her viral hit “Love Songs” in 2018. That success led to her first album Parked Car Convos (2019) followed up by Teenage Fever (2020), both filled with dreamy, emotional storytelling! Kaash continued to evolve with her 2022 project S2ML showcasing deeper songwriting and more confidence in her sound. By 2024 , she began moving independently, focused on creative freedom and ownership. In 2025, Kaash Paige Dropped a Trap Mixtape Titled “KAASHMYCHECKS” & A R&B/HIP HOP PROJECT Titled “2 Late To Be Toxic”. she has leveled up globally Going on her China Tour summer of 2025. headlining her second U.S. Tour “ Better than revenge “ & performing in Brazil at the Cena Festival in November 2025 . marking a new chapter in her career as an international artist. From SoundCloud uploads in Dallas to world stages across continents, Kaash Paige continues to prove why she’s one of the most refreshing and visionary voices in the world.
