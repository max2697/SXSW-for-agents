Summary

Would you trust AI to save your life? Join Motorola Solutions and Anthropic to explore designing AI for your worst day. Discover how rigorously boundary testing AI in life-or-death situations—where seconds save lives—reveals critical truths for AI safety. Unpack "good friction" and essential controls to prevent harmful AI, including automation bias. Learn frameworks for responsible human-AI collaboration applicable across any industry, building AI you can stake your life on.
