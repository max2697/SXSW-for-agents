Smarter Together: The Cognitive Toolkit for Better AI

Event ID: PP1162752

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Venue: Rio Grande Ballroom 1 (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As AI reshapes how we think, feel, and decide, this workshop explores how marketers, researchers, and creators can design more human-centered AI systems. Blending neuroscience, behavioral science, and real-world case studies, we’ll unpack the psychology behind trust, emotion, and collaboration in AI. Participants will leave with a framework for building AI that enhances—not replaces—human insight, helping teams navigate this new era with curiosity, empathy, and practical wisdom.
