Summary

We're first generation Indian-Americans from Texas, who grew up blending our Desi roots with our American culture through music and dance. With over 25 years of training in various dance styles such as Bollywood, Bharatnatyam, Kathak, Bhangra, Garba/Raas and Hip Hop., we came together and added a modern touch to our style, transitioning to Bollywood Fusion, Semi-Classical, Tollywood and more! After years of professional training, dance companies, dancing in UT Austin's Bollywood Dance team, Texas Mohini, and dancing professionally in Los Angeles, our journey has brought us back to Austin, where we were eager to keep our passion for dance alive. In 2023, bollyflow was born–a space to create fusing our unique dance styles and share the joy of dance with our community through workshops, events and wedding choreography!
