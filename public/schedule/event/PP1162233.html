Brand Strategy Workshop for Emerging Tech Companies

Event ID: PP1162233

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Venue: Rio Grande Ballroom 2 (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

This workshop is focused on the strategic brand positioning of your innovative company and cutting-edge technology. We will be firmly situated on the market side of product-market fit. We will start by investigating your target market(s) and then your competitive set. Then we will work together to articulate the qualities of your company and technology that are differentiated and valuable to your next investor or client. All with the goal of helping your company to stand out in an unprecedented time of innovation.
