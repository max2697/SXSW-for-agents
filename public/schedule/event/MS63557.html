Summary

Ximena Soto is an upcoming artist from Monterrey, México, and a rising voice in contemporary R&B. Her music blends modern R&B with nostalgic samples inspired by trending sounds from the 80s and 90s, shaping a fresh identity. Backed by established producers in the Mexican music scene, Ximena has fully found her sound and is currently working on an EP set for release next year. As an ambassador of the LGBTQ+ community, she delivers “hot music” rooted in freedom, self-expression, and emotional connection, reaching audiences beyond labels and genres.
