Slomo Drags

Event ID: MS63719

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 12:00 AM - 12:40 AM

Venue: Chess Club

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Slomo Drags are masters of bouncy indie-rock jams full of gorgeous vocal harmonies and gnarly, angular guitar work. The vibrant music is the perfect trojan horse for wildly irreverent lyrics featuring such characters as The Rich Pervert in the self driving car or The Repeater who corners you on a bar patio and subjects you to a never ending loop of the same three sentences. Slomo is kicking off 2026 with a string of single releases on Spaceflight Records, starting with "Fresh Bread" - a hypnotically catch tune for folks who love paranoia and malaise with their pop.
