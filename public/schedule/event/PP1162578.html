Brains & Bots: Evolving the Future of Intelligence Together

Event ID: PP1162578

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Salon D (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Does offloading thinking to AI cause cognitive atrophy or free our brains to think about more complex problems? That’s the million-neuron question. This provocative panel explores “adaptive intelligence”, where human and machine learning loops converge. A neuroscientist, futurist, and AI visionary unpack how co-learning systems, neuromorphic design, and spatial computing can amplify creativity, decision-making, and human agency in a world where algorithms don’t just support us, they shape us.
