Viva La Revolution: How the Digital Age is Transforming Wellness

Event ID: PP1149639

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Digital technologies have become indispensable in healthcare and wellness, touching every aspect of how we stay healthy and receive care today. As a community of innovators, we’ve already proven the viability of technologies such as wearables, telehealth, artificial intelligence, and mHealth. Join us in imagining how these technologies will look in the future and how they will synergize with one another in the digital health ecosystem of tomorrow. In this talk, we will discuss the latest advancements and policies that can accelerate the digital age of healthcare. We will delve more into specific technologies, exploring the promise they hold and the pitfalls we must watch out far as we use them to build the healthcare tools of tomorrow. Together we’ll learn how we build a digital future in which we live healthier and more fulfilling lives!
