Work Reimagined: Building Attraction, Not Requirements

Event ID: PP1148604

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Salon B (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Stop fighting about office days and start designing workplace experiences that people actually want. This panel brings together LinkedIn's workforce research leader with real-time employee insights, the architect of Gensler's leading workplace experience platform, and Rivian's expert in customer community building. Discover why meaningful in-person connection becomes even more valuable as AI handles routine work, cutting edge examples of experimental workplaces, and proven strategies to create immediately measurable ROI from thoughtfully designed workplace experiences and communities.
