Augmented Reality & Place-Based Learning: Layers of Place

Event ID: PP1162950

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:30 PM

Venue: Rio Grande Ballroom AB (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

This interactive workshop explores how augmented reality (AR) can be used as a pedagogical tool to deepen learners’ relationships to place, memory, and community. Drawing from Layers of Place: Austin—a collection of six location-based AR artworks—participants will engage with artists’ approaches to place-based storytelling, public history, and experiential learning. Through discussion, short activities, and case studies, the session examines how AR can function as a method for cultivating attention, care, and critical engagement with the spaces we inhabit.
