Summary

She23 is an Austin-native singer, rapper, DJ and songwriter known for blending genres and building culture at the same time. Fusing hip-hop, R&B, and alternative influences, She is known for high-energy, club-oriented tracks and local performances. Active in the ATX music scene for over 15 years, She released the "2H3EO" EP in 2024 and is known for singles like "Goofy Goober" & "Chedda". You can also find She23 spinning some of your favorite hits at local venues such as Coconut Club, Traphouse and many more. Beyond the music, she curates and coordinates events that spotlight community, collaboration, and emerging voices. She23 has stepped onto official stages for SXSW, Dream Con, and AfroTech, continuing to make her mark as both a genre-blending artist and a force behind the scenes.
