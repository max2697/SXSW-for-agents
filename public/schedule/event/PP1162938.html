Hello Again: Restoring Voices with AI

Event ID: PP1162938

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Salon D (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

What if you lost the ability to speak forever and then found it again? This session offers a preview of an 11-part docuseries spotlighting people living with permanent voice loss and how, through AI technology, they speak again to their family, friends, and now the world. During the panel, you’ll witness a woman use her AI-restored voice live on stage and learn how voices can be recreated from home videos or even a single voicemail. You will walk away with a deeper understanding of how this work expands access, restores identity, and helps shape a more human-centered AI future.
