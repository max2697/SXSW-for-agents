Beyond Branding: Advertising as Infrastructure for Change

Event ID: PP1162406

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 3 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Advertising is evolving—from persuasion to public service. Marco Venturelli explores how brands like AXA and McDonald's are turning campaigns into agents of change, from securing housing in contract fine print for domestic violence survivors to lighting dark city streets with golden arches. Showcasing powerful work from across the globe, he reveals how marketing budgets can solve real problems—not just build brands but build solutions.
