View on Official SXSW

Summary

Emerging from the heart of New York City’s underground music scene, Stare Away is the brainchild of Ben Nelson, a project that fuses post-punk intensity with a retro-coded aesthetic inspired by the dark alternative icons of the 1980s. Haunting melodies and brooding soundscapes channel the spirit of goth-driven acts from that era, offering a sonic journey steeped in existential reflection and melancholic beauty. A true embodiment of modern dark romanticism, Stare Away continues to captivate audiences seeking meaning in the shadows.
