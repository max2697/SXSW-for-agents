Summary

Growing up in Staghorn Flat, Victoria, Lucky burst onto the scene in flurry of fuzzy guitars and captivating storytelling. Blending the raw energy of alternative rock, with the dreamy haze of modern indie, her music captures the nuances of life's everyday moments and dives deep, putting you right at the centre of her world. Having supported the likes of The Belair Lip Bombs, Hockey Dad, Armlock and Dear Seattle, the Melbourne based artist is set for an exciting 2026 - following the release of her single ‘Lady Beetle’, Lucky is building a devoted audience who are eagerly waiting to see what’s in store next.
