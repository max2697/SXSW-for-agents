Unscripted Insiders: Netflix x Hulu Meet Up

Event ID: PP1150516

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Room 408 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Netflix’s Director of Unscripted Casting Rich Leist helped launch Netflix’s first in-house unscripted casting team, giving him a front-row seat to what platforms are actually looking for when they decide who gets on screen. Joining him is Matt Nagorner, formerly a Warner Bros. unscripted talent lead and now co-president of IM Trying Productions, where he develops personality-driven unscripted series and formats. This meet-up is designed for connection first: a quick, candid kickoff on what’s working in unscripted right now (casting, talent packaging, formats, and where the market is moving), followed by plenty of time for fans, aspiring cast, producers, and suppliers to meet each other, swap intel, and leave with new contacts. Along the way, Matt can pull back the curtain on working across major franchises like The Bachelor, The Voice, and event specials including **Friends: The Reunion and the **Harry Potter 20th Anniversary: Return to Hogwarts.
