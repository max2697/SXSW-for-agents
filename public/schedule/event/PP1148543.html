Event ID: PP1148543

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Room 303 (JW Marriott)

Summary

Connect with a global community of UX and AX designers and researchers shaping the future of digital experiences. Hosted by Cindy Brummer of Standard Beagle and UX in ATX, this popular meetup returns to SXSW to bring together creatives working across user-centered design and AI-enabled experiences. Whether you’re into product design, research, or agentic systems, join us to swap ideas, network, and get inspired. All experience levels welcome—come meet your people!
