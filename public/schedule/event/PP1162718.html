Floods to Fortitude: Infrastructure Resilience

Event ID: PP1162718

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Room 201-202 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Infrastructure is facing unprecedented challenges, from aging bridges to extreme weather events. This session will discuss how we can build better, more resilient infrastructure in an era of increasing volatility. It will feature findings from the Economist's Infrastructure for Good initiative, the American Society of Civil Engineers’ Infrastructure Report Card, and an upcoming report on infrastructure resilience. It will also explore the transformative role of technology, spotlighting Kentucky’s response to recent devastating floods.
