Summary

Are you an aspiring music industry professional or emerging artist? Come learn more about GRAMMY U, the Recording Academy's membership program for full-time college students, or those aged 18-29, pursuing a music career. GRAMMY U Representatives Ily Aguilar (National Projects Rep) and Mykel Merritt (Texas Chapter Rep) will kick things off by discussing the member benefits that come with GRAMMY U, which is celebrating its 20th Anniversary this year. Afterward, network with Recording Academy staff, Texas Chapter Board members, and fellow industry peers who are excited to take the next step in their music careers.
