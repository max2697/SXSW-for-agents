Summary

Joshua Ray Walker is one of Americana’s most arresting new voices. A Texas singer-songwriter whose powerhouse vocals, razor-sharp writing, and magnetic stage presence have earned praise from Rolling Stone, NPR, Billboard, and fans around the world. Known for his humor, heartbreak, and once-in-a-generation vocal range, Walker’s show pulls back the curtain on the characters, places, and moments behind his prolific and acclaimed discography. On stage, he’s warm, hilarious, and disarmingly honest, moving from roaring laughter to pin-drop-quiet confession in a matter of seconds. Joshua Ray Walker’s performances reveal him at his very best: raw, vulnerable, and unforgettable.
