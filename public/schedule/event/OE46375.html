Coca-Cola Sips & Sounds Music Festival

Event ID: OE46375

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 7:00 PM - 3:00 AM

Venue: Auditorium Shores

View on Official SXSW

Summary

<b>Sips & Sounds festival wristband required for entry; RSVP to claim your spot.</b> Coca-Cola Sips & Sounds Music Festival is a two-day music celebration that takes place every March at Auditorium Shores in the heart of Austin, Texas. Get ready for unforgettable performances from Christina Aguilera, Calvin Harris, Foster The People, and more among the city’s iconic skyline, plus refreshing Coca-Cola beverages and a curated selection of local food and drink to enjoy all weekend long. Visit <a href="https://www.sipssoundsfest.com/" target="_blank">https://www.sipssoundsfest.com/</a> for schedule and festival information. Access is limited to the first 1,000 SXSW Platinum badge holders to reserve for a wristband. You will receive a confirmation email if your reservation has been awarded. Bring a photo ID to claim your wristband at will call at Auditorium Shores (800 W Riverside Dr., Austin, TX 78704). Box office hours can be found here: <a href="https://support.sipssoundsfest.com/hc/en-us/articles/23564754662548-Where-is-the-box-office-What-are-the-hours" target="_blank">https://support.sipssoundsfest.com/hc/en-us/articles/23564754662548-Where-is-the-box-office-What-are-the-hours</a>
