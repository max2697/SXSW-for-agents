Everything Is a Touchpoint: Designing Cohesive Brand Moments

Event ID: PP1150126

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Venue: Guadalupe (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Every interaction tells your brand’s story. As marketing guru Seth Godin reminds us, “Every interaction, in any form, is branding.” This workshop explores how every facet of your brand, from your website to a digital campaign or even an internal memo, creates a lasting impression. Join us as we break down universal principles of elevated design, brand consistency, and intuitive user experience, drawing on real-world examples. This session blends inspiration with hands-on practice, equipping you with actionable methods for strengthening brand cohesion across every touchpoint you influence.
