Shifting From Beauty to Belonging—the Next Cultural Movement

Event ID: PP1162907

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Onyx Ballroom 1-4 (The LINE)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

For decades, beauty marketing rewarded aspiration, perfection, and the impossible. But consumers — especially multicultural and Gen Z audiences — have rejected that script. They want relief. relevance. representation. And a brand that actually sees their lived reality. In this provocative session, Kimberly Hairston-Hicks, CMO of Gold Bond and founder of Good Beauté, argues that the most powerful force reshaping consumer behavior today isn’t the beauty industry — it’s the Belonging Economy.
