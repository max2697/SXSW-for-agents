Big Ideas, Tiny Budgets: How Creativity Wins

Event ID: PP1162250

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom A (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Grace Wells breaks down how she turned scrappy parody commercials into a full-blown creative career. She’ll walk through her process for producing viral-quality product videos using minimal equipment, a sharp eye, and a lot of imagination. Attendees will learn how creativity, not budget, drives reach—and how to use humor, storyboarding, and craft to make content that brands and audiences love.
