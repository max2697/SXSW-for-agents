Summary

DD Island is a Brooklyn-based folk-rock band, formed in 2023 over a shared love of analog recording. Drawing on the familiarity of '60s and '70s West Coast country and psychedelic rock, DD Island still pulses with a brightness and grit that is grounded in present-day New York. The band’s debut LP, “Setting Sun” is due in Spring 2026.
