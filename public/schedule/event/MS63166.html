Summary

From the heart of Mexico City emerges BrunOG, an artist and producer who is reshaping the Urban Latin Pop landscape. With a solid trajectory within the industry, BrunOG has evolved from being a visionary producer—recognized even with a Latin Grammy nomination—to an established singer-songwriter with viral international impact. His image has always been synonymous with inspiration and aspiration. BrunOG’s musical proposal is a sonic blend rooted in Urban Latin Pop, yet it boldly ventures into genres such as Reggaeton, R&B, Afropop/Afrobeat, Hyperpop, Latin, and Alternative Pop. This versatility, however, always maintains a distinctive urban stamp in his lyrics, flows, and metrics, exploring themes of heartbreak, nostalgia, passionate love, sensuality, and romance. Milestones Global Viral Success: At 30, his track “Nuestra Canción” became an international phenomenon, driven by its inclusion in the soundtrack of the film “Culpa Mía.” Signing with Universal Music US Latin: In 2024, BrunOG joined the ranks of Universal Music US Latin, marking a new and exciting chapter in his career with multinational backing.
