Keynote: Nature Speaks. Can AI Help Us Listen?

Event ID: PP1149095

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Venue: Hilton Grand Ballroom (Salon HJK) (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

More than 8 million species share our planet. Until now, we’ve only understood the language of one. In this keynote, Aza Raskin, Co-founder of Earth Species Project, explores how AI is being used to decode the languages of other species—revealing complex, learned communication across the animal kingdom. For the first time in history, we can truly listen. The question isn’t if we’ll break the interspecies barrier. It’s whether we’ll do it in time to change everything.
