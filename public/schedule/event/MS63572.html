SRI

Event ID: MS63572

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 6:00 AM - 6:45 AM

Venue: Flamingo Cantina

View on Official SXSW

Summary

SRI is a Brooklyn-based DJ, producer, and artist crafting futuristic club music through a South Asian lens. Known for percussive, high-energy sets and hypnotic vocals, she builds immersive worlds that feel both cinematic and visceral. “SXSW is about discovery, and this showcase highlights how South Asian artists are shaping what’s next. I’m excited to bring my world, my sound, and my community into that space.” She will be featured as a performer for the SOUTH ASIAN HOUSE Soundvilla showcase for SXSW 2026. Leading up to her showcase performance, she will be releasing a single that fuses the worlds of Brooklyn, NY and Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
