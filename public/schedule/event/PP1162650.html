Access to Capital for the World's Underbanked Entrepreneurs

Event ID: PP1162650

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Room 412 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

"Access to Capital for the World's Underbanked Entrepreneurs" will be a creative discussion centered on how to expand financial access for underbanked and underserved entrepreneurs around the world – helping to create local jobs and economic mobility for founders from Detroit to Nairobi to the Za'atari refugee camp and beyond. Two award-winning social entrepreneurs and human rights leaders will discuss their work on the ground in the U.S., Africa, and Asia to connect women, refugee, and low-income individuals to the capital they need to launch and grow life-changing micro-enterprises.
