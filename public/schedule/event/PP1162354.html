Low-Key Legends: Personal Branding for Introverts

Event ID: PP1162354

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

This session with author of "Personal Branding for Introverts" and branding expert and ex-Forbes Sr. Contributor Goldie Chan focuses on empowering introverts to embrace their unique strengths to build a personal brand while struggling less. Attendees will learn how to harness quiet confidence for storytelling, develop strategies to connect with audiences without overwhelming social demands, and create a branding approach that aligns with their personality.
