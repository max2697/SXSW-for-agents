Summary

TTSSFU – aka Tasmin Nicole Stephens – quickly went from being a mere fixture in Manchester’s indie scene to one of its fastest-rising stars, now poised to break out well beyond it. Known for conjuring vast, atmospheric worlds from her bedroom using just Garageband, she watched as early releases like her Warhol-inspired 2024 EP “Me, Jed and Andy” picked up steam. Since then, she’s traded bedrooms for stages supporting Kim Deal, English Teacher, Soccer Mommy and Mannequin Pussy, plus sold out headlines across the UK and packed festival sets from Green Man to the Great Escape.
