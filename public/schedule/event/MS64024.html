Summary

Jacki Daniels is a Michigan-based, Nashville recording artist known for her powerhouse vocals, magnetic stage presence, and emotionally charged songwriting. In 2025, she was awarded Rising Star of the Year at the Josie Music Awards, the largest independent music awards show in the world, and received four Michigan Music Awards along with the Vehicle City Sound Podcast Fan Favorite Award two years in a row, establishing her as one of pop-country’s most promising new voices. Jacki’s versatility extends well beyond pop-country, with musical roots in rock, folk, a cappella, musical theater, R&B, electronic pop, indie, and classical. She performs everything from intimate solo acoustic sets to full band, high-energy shows, bringing a bold, genre-blending edge to every stage. She is the creator and host of The Jacki Daniels Show and co-host of Singers in the Round, a singer/songwriter showcase highlighting standout talent. Jacki also tours nationally with the Ultimate Kenny Rogers Tribute Show. Check her schedule for upcoming tour dates at www.thejackidanielsshow.com
