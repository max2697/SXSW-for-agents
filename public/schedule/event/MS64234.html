Summary

A cornerstone of Atlanta’s alternative scene, Kodie Shane is the seasoned architect of "Ethereal Trap." Evolving from a teenage prodigy into a fully autonomous force, she navigated the major-label system at Epic Records before successfully negotiating her independence. Today, she operates with 100% creative control and a "glitter-eyed" aesthetic that defies convention. A songwriter first, Kodie holds credits on 95% of her catalog, with over 100 releases and 100M+ streams to her name. Her raw vulnerability and melodic precision have made her a premier collaborator and a global touring staple. Whether headlining international dates from London to South Africa or supporting icons like Jhené Aiko, Lil Uzi Vert, and Willow Smith, her high-octane stage presence is unmatched. Beyond music, Kodie is a premier tastemaker cited by Vogue and Pitchfork. Blending luxury high fashion with streetwear, she has fronted global campaigns that challenge gender norms, solidifying her status as a visual icon for a loyal community of 850,000+ followers and 300,000 + monthly listeners on Spotify
