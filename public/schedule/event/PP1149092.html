The Numbers Behind the Hits: How Data is Driving K-pop's Global Domination

Event ID: PP1149092

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Capitol Ballroom ABCD (Downright Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

This exclusive industry panel brings together major Korean music labels and data analytics leaders to reveal the strategies behind K-pop's global dominance—and what Western music can learn from it. Hear how Korean labels leverage data to make decisions about tours, content, and promotion across different regions. We'll talk about the distinctive "superfan" cultivation approach that prioritizes deep engagement through multi-platform content funnels over traditional radio-and-playlist reach, and examine their export-first philosophy: building global audiences from day one with multilingual content and culturally-adaptive campaigns rather than establishing domestic success first.
