Summary

Tired of surface-level networking and awkward pitches? You’re not alone—53% of professionals say making real connections is their biggest challenge. Join this interactive, no-pitch Connect 4 Speakeasy, a collaborative space for creatives to engage deeply through real conversations, problem solving, and discovering the four allies every creative career needs. Learn to navigate networking strategically at SXSW and beyond to grow your audience and elevate your brand while connecting with people, not positions, to build authentic bonds.
