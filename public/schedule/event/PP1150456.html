Featured Session: A Waste of Time with Larry David and Jeff Schaffer

Event ID: PP1150456

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon 6-8 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

After unnerving the populace of the West Side of Los Angeles on Curb Your Enthusiasm, Larry David and Jeff Schaffer decided to cast a wider net on a grander subject - the History of the United States. In honor of America’s 250th anniversary they have teamed up with the Obamas and Higher Ground for a historical sketch comedy series debuting on HBO this summer. Larry and Jeff will discuss their process for Curb and the similarities and differences in making their new series - including a sneak peak of never-before-seen footage from the new show. And if you decide to walk out in the middle of it, they will completely understand. Moderated by Lorraine Ali
