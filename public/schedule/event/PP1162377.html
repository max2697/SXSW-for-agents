Design Trends That Matter

Event ID: PP1162377

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

It’s not news that we’re living in an era of transformation. But what is the role of design in shaping our future? And what is the responsibility of designers? The annual iF Design Trend Report seeks detailed answers. In this dynamic panel, three trailblazing design leaders come together to preview the international design institute’s top insights for 2026. Building on their decades of interdisciplinary expertise, panelists will explore intersectional topics such as autonomous vehicles, humanoid robots, emerging education, multisensory marketing, and much more. For those who love great design!
