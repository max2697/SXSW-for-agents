Cassettes & Cults: Making The Red Weather Podcast

Event ID: PP1163000

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom F (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Using the scripted podcast Red Weather as a focused case study, this session explores how narrative audio is developed from script to final performance, and why podcasts have become a powerful, creator-driven pathway for original IP. With creator Rider Strong and key cast members, the panel examines writing, performance, and collaboration, with a focus on practical, on-location recording in real environments — redwoods, diners, and moving cars — and how physical space shapes immersive audio storytelling.
