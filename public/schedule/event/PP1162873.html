Game On: Reimagining Career Exploration Through Play

Event ID: PP1162873

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 3:45 PM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 4 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Manufacturing is high-tech, high-paying, and full of opportunity—yet this vital industry still ranks last among teens’ career choices. By blending game-based learning, Dimensions of Success, and authentic career experiences shaped by SMEs and teachers, a futuristic learning pod is reshaping how students view advanced manufacturing. After one semester, students are 75% more likely to consider careers in the field, and 63% want further learning. Discover how a global industry leader, a nonprofit, and educators are sparking new enthusiasm and transforming perceptions of technical careers.
