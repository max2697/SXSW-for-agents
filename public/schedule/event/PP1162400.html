Event ID: PP1162400

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Summary

Step into the future with three XR and SXSW veterans as they pull back the curtain on the special 10th anniversary edition of the XR Experience, a SXSW highlight since 2016. Deep dive into cutting-edge immersive tech, storytelling, and AI innovations. Whether you’re a first-timer or a seasoned attendee, learn how to make the most of your time at the exhibition and connect with creators and industry leaders.
