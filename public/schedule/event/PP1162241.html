Beyond the Algorithm: Belonging in a Human+AI World

Event ID: PP1162241

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon B (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As AI becomes a collaborator at work, it’s reshaping how people find meaning, connect, and contribute. This panel explores how belonging evolves in Human+AI teams and the behaviors and mindsets leaders must model to inspire trust, motivation, and performance. Talent and leadership experts will discuss how to design emotionally intelligent cultures, the human skills that matter most in an AI world, and how to ensure people feel valued for what only they can uniquely bring to an AI-augmented workplace.
