International Syncs You're Missing Out on & How to Get Them

Event ID: PP1162350

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Capitol Ballroom FGH (Downright Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

There's a global sync market outside the US that is thriving. And there are key differences to understand if you want to reach them. This curated group of top international music supervisor panelists will discuss not only those key insights but also get practical about how you can tailor your approach to get the attention you need. Whether you're looking to pitch music or become an ad music supervisor yourself, this panel's intention is to be insightful and provide helpful tools for your next steps. The path isn't linear so all are welcome.
