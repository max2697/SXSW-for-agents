Summary

MITA — The Alchemist of Latin Pop Based in Mexico City, MITA is a producer, singer-songwriter, composer and DJ shaping a distinctive vision of contemporary Latin pop. Holding a Master’s degree in Social Sciences, she fuses intellect and instinct to read the collective pulse and translate it into refined yet playful Latin pop narratives infused with R&B, Kpop and tropical rhythms. A composer for Netflix and HBO, MITA moves fluidly between the club and the stage, delivering magnetic performances where connection, presence and storytelling place the audience at the center. Her debut album D.A.R (Desire, Love & Romance) frames love as a cyclical journey toward self-acceptance, positioning MITA as a multifaceted voice of modern Latin identity.
