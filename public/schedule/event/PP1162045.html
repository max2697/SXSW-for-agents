AI’s Rise, Talent’s Fall: Fixing the Skills Deficit

Event ID: PP1162045

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 10:20 AM

Venue: Waller Ballroom B (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Everyone’s talking about AI disruption — but not enough are talking about the human talent disruption it demands. This candid session challenges the myth that AI alone will future-proof workforces. It exposes blind spots in today’s upskilling agendas — from overlooked durable skills to the illusion of instant AI fluency. Backed by global insights and fresh data, this session offers a bold roadmap to turn AI anxiety into human potential — where curiosity, creativity, and adaptability matter as much as code.
