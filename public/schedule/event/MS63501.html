NOAMZ

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 2:10 AM - 2:40 AM

View on Official SXSW

Summary

NOAMZ is a flutist, vocalist, multi-instrumentalist, and producer making alt soul music. She was raised in a small town in Wisconsin by a Guadeloupean mother and French father. She went on tour in 2024 on her artist project, released her EP “Personne” August of 2025 with Guesthouse, and plays regularly across SoCal and the Midwest. And has recently grown in popularity online for her flute beatboxing. Featured on numerous LA based artist tracks, radios, and the Senegalese soccer anthem, she plans to release three musical projects this coming year, from her meditative flute project “Retrouvaille” to her soulful Melodic DnB EP “TEXTURES” dropping this March.
