J-Nice The Kingdom Builder

Event ID: MS63615

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 12:00 AM - 12:45 AM

Venue: Victory Grill

View on Official SXSW

Summary

J-Nice The Kingdom Builder, is an Austin, TX Christian hip-hop artist who has been creating music for over 20 years. He is the founder of Kingdom Builder Ministries. This movement inspires youth and young adults to use the arts to present the Gospel of Jesus Christ. J-Nice has released seven Mixtapes, over 20 Music Videos, published poems, hosted the longest-running Christian hip-hop showcase in the state of Texas, won Christian Hip-Hop Artist of the Year at the Kingdom Excellence Awards, and released 4 albums that have charted on Apple Music. He has been on platforms presented by the National Football League, MTV, BET, CBS, Spectrum News, and many more. This lyrical wordsmith makes it his duty to teach biblical truths with witty and clever wordplay, which is sure to leave listeners pleasantly surprised.
