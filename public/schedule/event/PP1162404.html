Tech-Free Creativity and Connection Meet Up

Event ID: PP1162404

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Room 408 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Take a break from the buzz of SXSW at our second annual Tech-Free Creativity and Connection Meet Up — Something to Write Home About edition. Unplug and create a one-of-a-kind postcard to your future self or someone special. With scratch art, embroidery, colored pencils, and more, this screen-free space invites artists, makers, and daydreamers to connect and create. Stamps will be provided so you can mail your creation or leave it to us to send out. Drop by anytime and stay as long as you like. Reflect, recharge, and create something special to write home about.
