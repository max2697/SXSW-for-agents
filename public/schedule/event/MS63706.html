DR. GRECO

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 11:00 PM - 12:30 AM

Summary

DR. GRECO, Rawsome Recordings & Odyssey Music Festival 20XX Founder. Since the inception of Rawsome Rave, his latest record label, he has quickly gained support from Patrick Topping, Patrick Mason, Interplanetary Criminal, Trym, Bad Boombox, Mija, David Löhlein, Justin Jay, JSTJR, t e s t p r e s s, Ollie Lishman, Mischluft, Maruwa, Benwal & many more! The debut release on his first label, Rawsome Recordings, titled 'Be' feat. Rowetta was a massive hit and quickly became a Summer anthem gaining support from Calvin Harris, Mau P, James Hype, Loco Dice, Nora En Pure, Sonny Fodera, Claptone, Seth Troxler + many more. His work also gained recognition from Billboard, Mixmag, DJ Mag, Dancing Astronaut, Insomniac & Complex. DR. GRECO has recently performed at EDC Orlando, We Belong Here & Space Miami. Rawsome has also hosted its own label events during ADE for the past 5 years, hosted an annual Summer boat party in New York City and currently hosts its own stage at OMF20XX each year. With early success and a string of exciting releases scheduled for 2026, DR. GRECO continues to push the boundaries of dance music into the future, all while preserving the roots of the underground.
