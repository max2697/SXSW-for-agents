Waste is the New Gold: How Trash Will Fund the Next Economy

Event ID: PP1162708

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Room 410 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

From wastewater to e-waste, the world is drowning in “garbage” that holds untapped value. But a new class of startups, scientists, and technologists is flipping the script by turning industrial runoff into rare earths, landfills into lithium banks, and toxins into trillion-dollar opportunities. This session will dive into how biology, chemistry, and data science are transforming global waste streams into the raw materials of the clean economy. Think of it as a startup gold rush just under our feet.
