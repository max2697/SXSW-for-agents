When the Future Dies: Ritualizing Grief + Joy

Event ID: PP1162467

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Building on last year's session 'Putting Endings in Our Beginnings', Juli Rush continues sharing her research on how we might ritualize both joy + grief as individuals and collectives to build more resilient systems. Bringing her work as a futurist, researcher at the University of Houston, and as a death doula, Juli shares both ancient and new rituals to employ within a chaotic and complex world. This work focuses on disrupting our business-as-usual world to build futures that see endings as new possibilities for the future.
