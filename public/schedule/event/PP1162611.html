Virtual (un)Reality – From Music to Immersive Storytelling

Event ID: PP1162611

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Room 410 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Can a song become a world? This panel explores how composer Philippe Cohen Solal transformed a music album into an expanded transmedia narrative. We’ll dive into the creation process to adapt music compositions to new forms of immersive interaction that led to the creation of the VR musical experience INSIDER-OUTSIDER. From DJ culture to tango revival, outsider art and virtual reality, we’ll discuss how culture remix and hybrid musical formats fuel new creative languages and audience engagement across physical and virtual spaces.
