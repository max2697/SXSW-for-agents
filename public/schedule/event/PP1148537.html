Summary

We’re drowning in biohacking hype. Billions of social media impressions promise easy health fixes and longer life, but how much is real? This panel cuts through the noise, separating fact from fiction for popular biohacks like vagus nerve stimulation and bioimplants. Join leading experts who debunk myths, reveal data-backed science and explore the REAL future of health optimization. Whether you’re managing chronic pain, seeking longevity, or striving for peak athletic performance, discover evidence-based strategies to boost your wellbeing in an era of misinformation.
