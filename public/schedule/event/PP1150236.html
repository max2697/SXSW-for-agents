Hidden Brilliance Revealed: Reimagining Leadership in a Fragmented World

Event ID: PP1150236

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Next Stage (Manchester Ballroom) (Fairmont)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Most leaders are taught to narrow themselves to fit the world. But what if innovation requires the opposite? In this intimate fireside chat, Tony Martignetti shares insights from his latest book, Illuminating Hidden Brilliance, revealing how reclaiming the sidelined parts of our identity is the key to creativity, connection, and transformative leadership. Through stories, research, and practical tools, Tony shows how multidimensional thinking becomes a catalyst and why the future belongs to leaders who bring all of themselves to the table. Attendees will learn why a multidimensional perspective is a strategic advantage, how to spot hidden brilliance in themselves and others, and how organizations can create environments where people can think and lead beyond the one-dimensional demands of modern work.
