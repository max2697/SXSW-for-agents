NIGHT DRIVE

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 4:00 AM - 4:40 AM

Summary

“Exactly the kind of glossy, echoed Interpol and Lower Dens vibes that are ideal to put on while rocketing down the interstate at 90mph in the dead of night.” — Spin Magazine Night Drive transforms cinematic sci-fi landscapes into high-impact synth-pop. Their emotive style fuses shadowed textures and reflective lyrics with soaring hooks and driving beats, creating a sleek, kinetic sound that has resonated across film, television, and radio worldwide. Touring alongside Crystal Castles, CHVRCHES, Ladytron, ACTORS and The Psychedelic Furs, the group has built a reputation for immersive live performances that resonate long after the final note. New releases arriving very soon.
