Orchestrate or Obsolete: Leading the AI-Powered Near Future

Event ID: PP1148931

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Salon AB (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The problem isn’t AI — it’s our leadership model. We’re still leading like humans are the only ones in the room… as the room quietly fills with AI agents. <br><br> In the very near future, every decision will be co-authored by humans, AI agents, and live ecosystem signals — and the leaders who master this orchestration will rewrite how business works.<br><br> Near Futurist Neil Redding shows how leadership must evolve in the Agentic Era, from command and control to the orchestration of human–AI symbiosis. You’ll leave with a clear mental model for leading in systems where humans and AI participate together, plus daily leadership rituals for thriving as business capabilities evolve in real time.
