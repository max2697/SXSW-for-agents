Technology Convergence: Winning the Exponential Upswing

Event ID: PP1162724

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Room 410 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Every industrial revolution started with mashups. Today, advanced digital, biological, and physical technologies are creating their S-Curves together, unleashing a burst of convergence that generates value none could release alone, a dynamic mapped by the World Economic Forum's Technology Convergence Report. In this hands-on session, executives and founders will work through a diagnostic framework to map the intricacies of today's technology landscape, stress-test where their next major bet sits within the convergence wave, and walk away with a practical lens they can apply immediately.
