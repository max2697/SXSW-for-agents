Creative AF: Women Creatives, Designers, & Content Hustlers

Event ID: PP1162652

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Room 410 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Whether you shoot it, write it, design it, sing it, or sell it - this space is for you. Creative AF (aka Women Who Create Sh*t) is a high-energy, no-BS meetup for women-identifying creatives, founders, filmmakers, freelancers, content hustlers, and brand builders who are making sh*t happen. Come for fast-paced connection games, resource swaps, and real talk & stay for the energy and photo-ops you’ll actually want to post. No panels. No gatekeeping. Just powerful, talented women building cool things and backing each other up.
