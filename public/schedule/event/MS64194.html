Summary

Nattali Rize is known for high energy, thought provoking and uplifting live performances, the powerhouse international band from Australia & Jamaica channel a deep world & reggae influence to present a one of a kind experience. With the long awaited sophomore studio album, LIBERATE, released worldwide on March 7th, the Nattali Rize band will be embarking on a global campaign of live shows and opportunities to connect with communities and people around the planet, this powerhouse act will be performing a headline Album Launch Tour in the USA followed by Europe, Australia, Japan and Beyond. “Nattali is a bright light in the world with a vision that extends beyond the stage.” - Michael Franti Her global anthem “One People” has amassed over 9 million views on youtube, Nattali and her group continue to connect across borders & waters with many highly acclaimed International festival appearances around the world. “One of the nation’s most refreshing voices” - Rolling Stone “INFECTIOUS!” - NPR
