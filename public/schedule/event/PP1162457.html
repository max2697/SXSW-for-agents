How AI is Driving the Future of Visual Work & Creativity

Event ID: PP1162457

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join Canva CCO Rob Giglio for a fireside chat on the collision between AI and visual creativity. As evolving technologies like AI have helped democratize design and content demands skyrocket, Rob reveals how visual work is evolving from exclusive expertise to universal expression. Learn why the future isn't AI replacing designers--it's AI empowering everyone to think visually, communicate effectively, and create impact at scale.
