Summary

Vin Zeal is a Houston, Texas–based artist blending the emotional depth of R&B with the infectious appeal of pop. Performing professionally since 2012, Vin Zeal has spent over a decade honing a sound rooted in authenticity, stage presence, and connection—bringing both polish and passion to every performance. Known for smooth vocals, melodic storytelling, and live guitar accompaniment, Vin Zeal creates music that feels both timeless and contemporary. Her ability to seamlessly incorporate guitar into her performances adds a dynamic, intimate edge to both recorded and live experiences. With extensive live performance experience and a clear artistic vision, Vin Zeal continues to build a compelling catalog that bridges pop accessibility with R&B soul—positioning her as a dynamic and evolving artist with longevity in mind.
