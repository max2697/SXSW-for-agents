Subpar Snatch

Event ID: MS63653

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 9:40 PM

Venue: The 13th Floor

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Subpar Snatch is a fierce, unapologetic punk rock powerhouse from Austin, Texas, delivering high-energy anthems that punch as hard as they play. Known for blistering bass riffs, riotous rhythms, and unapologetically raw lyrics, the band turns every stage into a sweaty, cathartic release for the crowd. These self-proclaimed shit asses draw on influences like Riot Grrrl and modern garage punk, Subpar Snatch blends ferocity with fun — their live shows are equal parts mosh pit and inside joke, fueled by razor-sharp wit and pure, unfiltered passion. They’ve built a loyal following through electrifying performances at local venues, roller derby bouts, and DIY festivals, creating an inclusive space for rebels, misfits, and anyone who wants to scream along. With a reputation for turning chaos into community, Subpar Snatch is ready to bring their high-octane, Texas rebellion to a stage near you. "Subpar" is anything but.
