Summary

How is HEINEKEN transforming itself into the world’s best‑connected brewer? Malcolm Gladwell and HEINEKEN’s Chief AI Officer, Surajeet Ghosh, sit down for a live episode of Smart Talks with IBM podcast to explore how the company is using data, AI and strong partnerships to create better experiences for customers and consumers worldwide. Hear how HEINEKEN is modernizing its digital backbone, empowering its people, and unlocking new ways to innovate in the future of beer.
