Build Community Through Storytelling

Event ID: PP1150392

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Room 410 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The fastest way to create community isn’t networking. It’s shared humanity. In this highly interactive session, FABx founder Colleen Schell and guests guide you through a storytelling experience designed to take the room from strangers to a connected community. You’ll explore why story is more than entertainment: it’s a cultural technology that dissolves separation, builds trust quickly, and invites people to belong. Learn how to share your most potent story and walk away with a powerful tool for connection. This isn’t a panel. It’s an experiment in connection through sharing your story.
