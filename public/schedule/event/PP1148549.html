The LEGO Futures Imaginarium: Play Your Way to Tomorrow

Event ID: PP1148549

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 4:30 PM

Venue: Brazos I-III (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

What if your most imaginative future was already inside you, hidden in your most cherished moment of childhood play? The LEGO Imaginarium is a hands-on foresight workshop where participants use LEGO bricks, storytelling, and memory prompts to reconnect with their inner child and build physical models of possible futures. Guided by futures thinking tools, the LEGO system in play, and inspired by the creativity of kids, this session blends strategic foresight with playful, hands-on exploration to unlock fresh perspectives on what’s next in the future.
