Prune or Perish: Cut Toxic Leadership for a Thriving Culture

Event ID: PP1162225

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Toxic leaders can look successful on the surface while quietly poisoning teams and cultures. This research-backed session reveals how to spot early warning signs of toxic leadership, recognize eight common toxic personas, and take courageous action to prevent damage. Learn how to build a healthier, more human-centered workplace where trust and engagement thrive—and where toxic behaviors are addressed before they take root.
