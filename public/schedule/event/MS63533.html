Summary

A prolific songwriter & soulful artist blending country, folk, & pop with witty, emotionally rich storytelling, "hippie songbird" BANDANA CHEYENNA is one to watch in today's music landscape. Already known as one of Nashville's hitmakers, their notable writing credits include Dasha’s global billion-stream hit “Austin (Boots Stop Workin’)," Alexandra Kay’s viral track "Cupid's A Cowgirl,” & more. As an artist, Cheyenna has released two full albums, & most recently Act I of a four-part EP series, "The Artist's Pilgrimage," which was celebrated with a sold-out release party on Music Row. Cheyenna has appeared on radio home of the Grand Ole Opry WSM-AM, performed at Nashville's Pride Festival & legendary Bluebird Café, & is gearing up for a performance on Austin’s major TV network coinciding with their SXSW appearance. A winner of Female Song of the Year at the 2024 People’s Choice Country Awards, & honoree at the 2024 & 2025 ASCAP Awards, their collaborative work includes artists & producers Gayle, Abby Anderson, Breland, Caylee Hammack, Dan Wilson (Chris Stapleton, Adele), Jonah Marais (Why Don’t We), MaRynn Taylor, Mickey Guyton, Nathan Chapman (Taylor Swift), Tigirlily Gold & more.
