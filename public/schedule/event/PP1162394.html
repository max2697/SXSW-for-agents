Producing Without Borders: Navigating Global Film

Event ID: PP1162394

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Mansfield AB (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

From Hollywood to Helsinki, film and TV productions are chasing the best talent and shooting locations — but what happens when creativity meets border control? This panel dives into the real-world challenges of global filmmaking, from navigating immigration laws and work permits to managing international crews — all within a tight budget and timeline. Join experts from the immigration and entertainment industries as they explore how the entertainment industry is adapting to a borderless future.
