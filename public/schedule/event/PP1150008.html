Music Managers Forum Meet-Up

Event ID: PP1150008

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Music Clubhouse (Downright Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Meet the Managers is a must-attend gathering for music managers at SXSW, whether you’re a seasoned industry veteran or just starting out. Hosted by Music Managers Forum-US (MMF-US), the leading trade association for music managers in the U.S. and a proud member of the IMMF global network, this event brings together managers from around the world to connect, exchange insights, and discuss key issues shaping the business of artist management. MMF-US executives and board members will be on hand to share updates on advocacy, membership, and initiatives, and to connect with the global manager community.
