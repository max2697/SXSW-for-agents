Casket Cassette

Event ID: MS63157

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 6:00 AM - 6:50 AM

Venue: Elysium

View on Official SXSW

Summary

A reflection of the industrial grime and perpetual sunny glow of Los Angeles, Casket Cassette (Constant Williams) blends euphoria and melancholy in an expression of existential perseverance. Casket Cassette's depressingly danceable music takes a contemporary spin on the dark sounds of the 1980s Post-Punk and Darkwave movements, drawing further influence from New Wave, EBM, Synthpop and Indie/ Alternative to achieve this duality. The project takes its name from a memory Williams has from childhood of his father recording a live song onto a cassette tape, then placing the tape into his grandfather's casket as it was being lowered into the ground. Williams' lyrics often reflect upon similarly somber themes such as mental health struggles, loss, heartbreak, addiction and recovery, while also offering glimmers of hope, humor and a general determination to overcome. Williams lives in LA, and graduated in 2024 from the lowa Writers' Workshop with an MFA in poetry.
