Summary

Era Wadi is an artist, rapper, and songwriter based in Austin, TX. His early musical inspirations began from his father's collection of soul and rock vinyls of the 60s/70s and hip hop of the late 90s/early 2000s. Era Wadi continues to build up his own discography and is releasing music independently. His constant aim at originality and being authentically himself attracts new audiences and has an exciting new EP releasing this Spring. When he’s not making music, you will find him hosting The Wadi Show with his DJ, Lil Em on Twitch + YouTube, a platform for artists by artists that has become one of the top live streaming channels for entertainment. Era Wadi’s unique voice, relatable songwriting, infectious energy, and impeccable beat selection, insure Wadi will carve out his place as an innovator in this new and ever evolving era of culture.
