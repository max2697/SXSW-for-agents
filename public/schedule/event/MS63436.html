DOSSEY

Event ID: MS63436

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 1:00 AM - 1:40 AM

Venue: Zilker Brewing

View on Official SXSW

Summary

DOSSEY started out making squeaky clean pop music in 2017 (NPR’s All Songs Considered actually referred to her in 2018 as “a joyous banquet of roséwave electro-pop”). But by now in 2025, the project has mutated into something brutal, noisy, honest, and unhinged. Motherhood, a slow unspooling of her faith, and the relentless static of American political and social climate have all pushed her toward the genesis of her own brand of dance punk - glitter grunge. So now, DOSSEY’s world hinges on distortion, punk rhythm and anthemic rock vocals influenced by The Clash, Nine Inch Nails and the Yeah Yeah Yeahs.
