Summary

Missouri native willoh is riding the crest of a wave of excitement over her new series of singles leading up to her spring 2026 album release. On her latest track, EARMILK writes ““OUCHIE” defies easy categorization, blending synth-pop, raw punk attitude, and alt-rock vibes into a shifting, avant-garde sound.” Willoh’s self-produced 20205 debut, not more than that, but more please presents us with a raw honesty rendered through snowy vocals that sit in rarefied air and belies the artist’s 19 years. In September she released the labyrinthine “Buckshot” to critical acclaim. From her home in Springfield, Missouri, willoh records and creates music, having first found her footing on Garageband before graduating to LogicPro–making the upgrade from her earnings working many long shifts at McDonalds.
