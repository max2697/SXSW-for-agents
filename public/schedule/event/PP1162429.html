Money, Sex & Religion - How Design Turns Taboo to Touchpoint

Event ID: PP1162429

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Salon D (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Sex, money, and religion are no longer taboo—they’re trending. This panel explores how behavioral UX, gamification, and human-centered design can destigmatize what used to be off-limits. From intimacy apps to investing platforms, discover how intentional design is helping users navigate vulnerable moments with confidence. Hear from sex educator Shan Boodram, Alinea’s Gen Z fintech founder Anam Lakhani, Bloomberg’s Riley Griffin, and Play Ventures’ Phylicia Koh—one of few female investors in gaming VC—on how intentional UX is turning discomfort into discovery.
