Vibe Shift: How New Media Upended Our Ideas and Aesthetics

Event ID: PP1162408

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom A (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

What are the vibes? This presentation will unpack the idea of a "vibe" - an aesthetic appraisal of a situation - and how these vibes can "shift." These shifts are dictated by cultural and political undercurrents, by current events, but most importantly by developments in technology. In a rapidly changing landscape with more sophisticated generative AI and social media algorithms, the vibes are shifting almost too fast to keep up. New meme genres, political movements, and ideological upheavals are occurring - and this talk will explain how and why.
