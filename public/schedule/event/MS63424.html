Summary

Lola Tried is a fast-paced, loud-as-hell rock band from Austin, Texas, with driving guitars, sharp melodies, and the commanding voice of Lauren Burton. Their sound blends snarling guitars, punchy rhythms, and an emotional edge that swings between chaos and control. With a relentless live presence and hooks that hit like a gut punch, Lola Tried channels the messy urgency of feminine rage through a sharp, modern lens.
