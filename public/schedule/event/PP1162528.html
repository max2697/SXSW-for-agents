It’s Fun Proving People Wrong. Turn Adversity into Success.

Event ID: PP1162528

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Salon D (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

If you’ve ever been underestimated or overlooked you’ll know that success is the best revenge. Madison Tevlin (a trailblazer with Down syndrome who was told at birth by doctors she wouldn’t amount to much) and Ashley Callingbull (who is a First Nations Activist and the first Indigenous Sports Illustrated Model) will share stories of how they used haters, doubt, and being underestimated as even stronger fuel for chasing their dreams and creating change. This session is for anyone who believes in breaking barriers, redefining limits, and proving the seemingly impossible is in fact possible.
