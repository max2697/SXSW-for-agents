Summary

Niiasii is an alternative pop artist from Southeast Virginia pushing the boundaries between pop, R&B, and alternative sounds. Her music is fueled by true emotion, a mix of love, heartbreak, and the relentless pursuit of freedom. On stage, Niiasii is electric. Turning vulnerability into energy, and every performance into a deep connection with the audience. With shows across the East Coast, she’s created her own lane for genre- bending music and unapologetic expression One of Niiasii's notable accomplishments is her music project titled "The Art Of Changing Form," which has gained significant recognition with over 100,000 streams. Since that release, Niiasii has gone on to deliver 3 more cohesive projects that have solidified and deepened her community of supporters. As the buzz continues to build, this emerging alt-pop talent stands firmly on the path to breakthrough success.
