Summary

lucky break is a 24-year-old New York-born indie rock artist based in San Francisco. Blending classic alt-rock, punk, and modern indie pop with a knack for homegrown radio hooks, the Biggest Thing EP marks lucky break's first official release. The collection, poised to establish the artist as a significant new voice in music, was recorded with Elliot Woodbridge (Ha Vay) in a makeshift car studio, each track offering a glimpse into the mysticism and whimsy of her expansive sonic world. And it’s just the beginning: Her full-length debut will release May 2026.
