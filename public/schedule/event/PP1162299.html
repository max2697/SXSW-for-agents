Inside the International Scam Syndicate: How We Fight Back

Event ID: PP1162299

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon D (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Scamming is a multibillion-dollar, transnational business rivaling legitimate enterprises. In actual offices, their workers labor leg to leg at long tables -- cubicles would actually be more humane. Scams are now a way for nation-states to steal the wealth of Americans, at speed and scale. These criminals are on a rampage, and those in power, in the US and abroad, aren't really focused on stopping it. Hear from a former prosecutor hell-bent on disrupting this prolific crime, an expert in deception, and a scam survivor and podcaster about how we can all fight back.
