Build the Future: Entrepreneurship Within Reach

Event ID: PP1148571

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Salon E (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Entrepreneurship is one of the last viable paths to economic mobility in America—offering ownership, stability, and long-term wealth. But that path is narrowing fast. Early-stage funding to women and underrepresented founders has dropped nearly 50% since 2020 (Crunchbase, 2023), while systems like childcare, capital access, and mentorship remain fragile. This session brings together cross-sector leaders to ask: What must we build now to keep entrepreneurship within reach for the next generation?
