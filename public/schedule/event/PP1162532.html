Foresight Improv

Event ID: PP1162532

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Salon E (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Do you wish you could predict the future? Come challenge our panel of futurists as they try to do it themselves! In 2024, SXSW attendees put a panel of futurists’ knowledge to the test, challenging them to improvise their view on the future of 18 audience-selected topics from toilet paper to war to thinking. Attendees submitted ideas into a fishbowl that spurred a constructive view of the future of a topic, in 3 minutes or less. This year we hope to return bigger and better with some new tricks and twists. Expect a rousing, dynamic, fun-filled adventure into the future and beyond!
