Featured Session: The Three Laws of a Healthy Relationship

Event ID: PP1149798

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Salon 1-4 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Yung Pueblo, Jillian Turecki, and Case Kenny break down the three laws of a healthy relationship in a conversation that blends self-awareness, communication, and what it truly means to love someone. Explore how connection with yourself sets the tone for intimacy, how optimism shapes the language of conflict, and why lasting love is a practice... not just butterflies or chemistry. Expect practical tools, perspective shifts, and real guidance for building relationships that last. A powerful, grounded session for anyone ready to strengthen how they love and how they show up in partnership.
