Pitches to Pipelines: Accelerating Circular Water Economy

Event ID: PP1162889

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Next Stage (Manchester Ballroom) (Fairmont)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The circular water economy is reshaping how we manage water, energy, and resources. This session highlights how innovation, financing, and collaboration drive the shift from linear to circular systems while building the next generation of water leaders. It will also spotlight WEF’s Circular Water Economy 101 course as a model for advancing education and innovation.
