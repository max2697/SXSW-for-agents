Summary

fifi knifefight is an Austin based glam punk band known for a powerful live presence and raw sound. Blending punk grit with theatrical flair, this is a must-see act for fans of Hole. Fronted by dynamic lead, the eponymous fifi knifefight, their performances often feature driving rhythms, unapologetic lyrics, and an in-your-face style that's intense yet captivating. Tracks like "joanie" and "THE PARTY" showcase their unique mix of emotional depth and defiance. Often found in downtown Austin and recently featured in several local festivals, fifi knifefight continues to make waves in the indie circuits.
