Rethinking Discovery Algorithms for the Creator Economy

Event ID: PP1162453

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

On most platforms, discovery is designed to maximize ad revenue — not creator success. But what if algorithms prioritized creator growth and authentic relationships instead? In this panel, Drew Rowny (VP of Product at Patreon), Dr. Rumman Chowdhury, PhD, and Taylor Lorenz (founder of User Mag and host of the Power User podcast) will unpack how discovery tools can be redesigned to support creators’ businesses first.
