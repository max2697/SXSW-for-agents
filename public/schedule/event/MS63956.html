SWDEJAY

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 9:45 PM

View on Official SXSW

Summary

SWDEJAY (Southwest Ejay) is a first-generation Mexican-American DJ from Southwest Detroit, deeply rooted in the city’s rich musical legacy. Known for his high-energy, open-format style, he seamlessly blends Latin House, Reggaeton, Dembow, Baile Funk, and Ghetto Tech — reflecting both his heritage and Detroit’s forward-thinking sound. As the curator of Fiesta House, he has played a key role in shaping Detroit’s Global Latin club scene, holding multiple residencies at some of the city’s top nightlife venues while building a cultural bridge between Detroit and the world. His sound has reached international dance floors through performances and productions in Paris, Berlin, London, Chicago, Guadalajara, and Mexico City. A true ambassador of global music, SWDEJAY continues pushing boundaries — bringing Detroit energy, Latin rhythm, and worldwide club culture together wherever he plays.
