Plantman & Blondie: A Dress Up Gang Film

Event ID: FS19413

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 11:15 AM - 12:53 PM

Venue: Alamo Lamar 1 (Alamo Lamar)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

An offbeat comedy about friendship, purpose, and trying to grow something real in a city built to keep people apart. Cory is a lonely, doomscrolling thirty-something whose most consistent relationship is with his Instagram algorithm. His apartment? An emotionally vacant, sterile, void. His fridge? Mostly Celsius. And his idea of connection is dodging calls from the one person who still tries: Fox, a soft-hearted, ex-con with terrible advice rooted in institutional paranoia and his love of films. Everything shifts when Cory has a run-in, literally, with Plantman, a rogue horticultural crusader armed with a mysterious dossier of negligent plant owners.
