Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 11:00 PM - 11:40 PM

Summary

MINORI out of Kingston Jamaica, dubbed 'Mini from the block', has arrived with a freshness. Tough yet full of soul, she bends Hip Hop, Reggae, RNB, Dub and Dancehall into something different, connecting with people at the core level. MINORI has been touring Nationally through out the USA since 2021 gracing festival stages such as Reggae Rise Up, Cali Roots, Montana Hemp Fest and opening for internationally acclaimed artists such as Nattali Rize and J-Boog taking audiences by storm with mesmerizing performances. Her epic live show is set on the wings of her masterful lyrical content and powerful vocal talent, winning hearts everywhere she steps. With her 2021 debut single release RED PILL she pushed Hip Hop / Trip Hop realms amassing attention with her fresh original style. “Play it loud! I definitely give this one the #RebelMusic stamp of approval. Not often you see this style of lyrical delivery by a sistren, so it’s very refreshing for me. I’ll be anticipating what else is in store” - Kabaka Pyramid "There’s an undeniable presence and power in MINORI’s lyrics and vocal flow" - Nattali Rize
