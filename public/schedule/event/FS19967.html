Sinner Supper Club

Event ID: FS19967

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 12:00 PM - 1:10 PM

Venue: Violet Crown 4 (Violet Crown Cinema)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Nora is late. Alice unceremoniously brought her partner, whose name no one can remember. Jayae doesn't want to be around "these" people...but came anyway? And Genevieve, the host, has put an unhealthy amount of pressure on this eviction funeral (kinda cringe, right?) to solve all of their problems. As they barrel through the evening, this disintegrating friend group, delirious from the heat and lack of food, desperately throws Hail Marys, caution to the wind, and lamb against the wall, trying to harness some semblance of what they used to have. ...and then their dead friend shows up.
