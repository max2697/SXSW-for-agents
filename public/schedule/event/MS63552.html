The Gringos

Event ID: MS63552

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 6:00 AM - 6:50 AM

Venue: Continental Club

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The Gringos are an indie rock band building a real-world scene. Born from house shows, late-night living room sessions, and college basements, their sound blends raw indie rock energy with melodic songwriting and a loose, lived-in Southern warmth. Their music feels like something you stumbled into — loud enough to feel alive, honest enough to feel personal. The Gringos aren’t just a band — they’re building a community. From packed college rooms to growing regional tours, their shows feel less like concerts and more like gatherings. The kind where strangers become friends, choruses get screamed back, and everyone leaves feeling like they found something. If you’re looking for polished pop — look somewhere else. If you’re looking for your new favorite band before everyone else finds them — welcome.
