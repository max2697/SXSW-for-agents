Redbreast Unhidden Showcase

Event ID: FS19982

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Venue: Alamo Lamar 5 (Alamo Lamar)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

In partnership with SXSW Film & TV Festival 2026, Redbreast Single Pot Still Irish Whiskey invites you to join in an unforgettable cinematic experience. Accompanied by an iconic Redbreast cocktail and sweet treats, we’re shining a light on five exceptional short films, hand-picked by award-winning Irish actor Andrew Scott. Bold and original, these five shorts represent the best of what the artform has to offer. Attendees must be over 21 and be SXSW badge holders. Entry is not guaranteed and permitted on a first come first serve basis.
