Innovation for Everyone: Day 2 at German Haus

Event ID: OE46590

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 5:30 AM

Venue: Speakeasy

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Dive into a day of deep insights and strategic networking at the German Haus. Day 2 showcases the versatility of the German ecosystem, featuring expert perspectives on MediaTech and high-level discussions from the Health and MedTech sectors. The afternoon is dedicated to building bridges: explore cutting-edge solutions from energy and sustainability innovators and discover the strength of Germany’s ties with Europe and the US. A highlight of the day is our special collaborative program with the European Union, fostering transatlantic dialogue. Wrap up the evening with a premium selection of drinks and a showcase of the finest new music Germany and Europe have to offer. <br><br> For more information please visit: https://www.german-haus.com
