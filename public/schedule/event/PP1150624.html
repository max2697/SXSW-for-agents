Breaking a Band in 2026: The Girl Tones Blueprint

Event ID: PP1150624

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Capitol Ballroom E (Downright Austin)

Summary

What does it actually take to break a new act in 2026, without a legacy playbook? This session brings together Girl Tones (sister duo Kenzie & Laila), Daniel Oakley, co-founder of the indie label Parallel Vision (with Brad Shultz of Cage The Elephant and Darren Potuck), and Nicole Rich, Director of Streaming at Big Loud Rock, Parallel Vision’s label partner, for a candid, tactical walkthrough of a modern artist-development model.
