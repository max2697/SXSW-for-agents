PR & Media Professionals Meet Up

Event ID: PP1150326

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Room 303 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join fellow sports, entertainment, and brand communications professionals for a PR and media meetup during SXSW, hosted by Tony Fay Public Relations. This gathering brings together publicists, media strategists, brand leaders, journalists, producers, and marketers working at the intersection of culture, live events, and storytelling. Designed as a networking opportunity, this meetup is part of the SXSW experience and brings together peers working across sports, entertainment, and brand communications. Attendees can expect engaging conversations around media strategy, brand storytelling, partnerships, live events, and how PR continues to evolve in an increasingly real-time, content-driven landscape. Whether you’re looking to expand your network, exchange ideas, or reconnect with industry colleagues, all sports, entertainment, and brand PR and media professionals are welcome.
