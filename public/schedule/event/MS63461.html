Summary

Los Gran Reyes are a Dallas-based nine-piece cumbia powerhouse led by Agustín Granados Jr. (AG), blending traditional cumbia styles with modern and electronic influences. Drawing from Cumbia Lagunera, Sonidera, and Argentina, their sound is high-energy, culture-driven, and built for the dance floor. Their latest album, Cumbia Vibes, marks a bold new era of cumbia moderna and solidifies Los Gran Reyes as one of the most exciting live Latin acts today.
