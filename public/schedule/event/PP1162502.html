Building Brands That Back Creators

Event ID: PP1162502

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 3 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As creators become the cultural vanguard, brand marketing must evolve. Author of the ICYMI newsletter Lia Haberman joins Mike McGarry, SVP of Marketing at Patreon, in a conversation on how to build campaigns that don’t just feature creators — they center them. Learn actionable insights from Patreon's marketing strategy which amplifies authenticity, drives relevance, and builds loyalty across both creator and fan communities. Through viral case studies and creator-led activations, this session will arm marketers, CMOs, and creatives with the tools to thrive in a world where creators are the brand.
