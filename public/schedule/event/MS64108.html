Summary

Attalie is a California-based music seamstress whose sound lives at the intersection of jazz, alternative R&B, and neo-soul. Her music serves as a sanctuary — a space where listeners are invited to let their guard down, look inward, and reconnect with themselves, empowered to be and become in spite of societal norms. Her work has received critical acclaim and has been featured by The Guardian, The FADER, CLASH, Mixmag, BBC Radio 1Xtra, KEXP, KCRW, NTS Live and more. She is currently unveiling a new chapter of her work led by two visual singles, Carry Me and Light, both offering an intimate glimpse into the sonic and visual world of her upcoming project, set for release in 2026.
