Unlocking Soccer's Development in the U.S.

Event ID: PP1149240

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

With the FIFA World Cup 26 kicking off in less than 100 days, the time is now for the most popular game globally to reach its full potential stateside. The world’s biggest sporting event has the opportunity to elevate soccer in the US to new heights and build future sustainability for growth far beyond the event. Join former USMNT star Jozy Altidore, City Football Group / Manchester City CMO Nuria Tarre and FIFA World Cup 26 NYNJ Host Committee CEO Alex Lasry for an insightful conversation on the future of the soccer in the United States. In this session, these three notable and unique voices will share perspective on the opportunities and efforts underway to optimize the future of soccer, from fan growth to player development, as well as brand and community engagement.
