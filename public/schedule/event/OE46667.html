Vibrating Music Hammocks

Event ID: OE46667

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 5:00 PM - 3:00 AM

Venue: Film & TV Clubhouse

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The Music Hammock transforms its fabric into a speaker, resonating your body with the frequencies of the music so you feel as if you’re lying inside a sound system. In our fast-paced society, we often disconnect from our bodies. This installation offers a full-body listening experience, shifting your attention from mental chatter into physical awareness. Experience sound not only through your ears, but through your entire body. <p> https://www.parkerhertzberger.nl/portfolio-collections/my-portfolio/project-title-6 <p>
