From Data to Decisions: Lives, Livelihoods, and the Planet

Event ID: PP1150101

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Room 203-204 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Decisions about land, conservation, food and energy systems, and climate are increasingly shaped by data and AI. But how does that information actually enter a decision? How does a farmer decide when to plant, or a policymaker weigh risk and uncertainty? This fireside chat explores how data-driven insights move from analysis to real-world decision-making across very different contexts, and what it takes for data and models to be reliable enough to act on when the stakes are high.
