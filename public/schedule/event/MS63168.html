Summary

Pretty Jane is a Detroit-born, Nashville-based indie rock/pop trio comprised of Trevor Dalrymple (vocals), Luca DiVergilio (bass), and Ethan Strain (lead guitar), who met after dating the same set of twins. They are known for their intimate and energetic music, which has garnered them over ten million streams and a large online following. Their sound is often described as a blend of alternative and indie with pop appeal, creating music that explores themes of intimacy, connection, and the complexities of relationships earning them the moniker 'music that feels like kissing your friends'.
