View on Official SXSW

Summary

"Baby/Girls" is a verité documentary set in Arkansas that explores what teen motherhood looks like in post-Dobbs America. Filmed over two years with unprecedented access inside a Christian maternity home for pregnant teens, the film follows three young women as they carry their pregnancies, give birth, and adapt to their new realities. Through their stories, the film offers a rare, unfiltered portrait of life at the intersection of girlhood and motherhood in the American South.
