Building a New, Future-Proof University in 2026

Event ID: PP1162126

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 3:45 PM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 5-6 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

What happens when an academic's vision for transforming higher education meets building an actual university? This session explores the journey from Alternative Universities' theoretical framework to building a new university in 2026 that is debt-free and apprenticeship-based. We examine the "ROTC for private sector" funding approach that shifts costs from students to employers, polymathic curriculum combining three non-adjacent majors, and navigating 2026's unique obstacles like accreditation and funding while leveraging opportunities from starting without entrenched bureaucracy.
