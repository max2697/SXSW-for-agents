Breaking the Sound Barrier: Making Creativity Accessible to All

Event ID: PP1162951

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Creators face a growing “sound barrier”: a fragmented ecosystem of tools, platforms, and services that often makes creating feel more complicated than it should be. What should be intuitive and fluid can become complex, costly, and disconnected. This panel explores what a more unified, creator-first space for music and podcast creation could look like that meets creators where they are in the journey. Whether its beginners or more experienced creators refining their craft, we’ll explore more streamlined solutions for music and podcasts that support creators at every stage.
