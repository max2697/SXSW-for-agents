Shaping Education in a Culture x Tech World

Event ID: PP1161631

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Guadalupe (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

At the intersection of art and technology, education is shifting from instruction to experimentation. This interactive Meet Up focuses on non-linear, learner-driven approaches where technology acts as a creative collaborator—not a skill to master. Through facilitated discussion, we’ll examine how knowledge emerges when disciplines collide and learning grows from uncertainty rather than fixed structures.
