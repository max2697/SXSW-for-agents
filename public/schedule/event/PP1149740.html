Featured Session: Cast and Creators on HBO's ROOSTER with Steve Carell, Matt Tarses, Bill Lawrence, and more

Event ID: PP1149740

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Salon 6-8 (JW Marriott)

Summary

Join the cast and creators behind the HBO Original comedy series ROOSTER, from Warner Bros. Television, for a conversation about the making of the show. Panelists include co-showrunners, executive producers, and writers Bill Lawrence ("Ted Lasso,” “Shrinking”) and Matt Tarses ("Scrubs"), along with series stars Steve Carell (“Greg Russo”/EP), Charly Clive (“Katie Russo”), and Danielle Deadwyler (“Dylan Shepherd”).
