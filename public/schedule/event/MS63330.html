Summary

THE BURES BAND BIO The Bures Band, is a fresh and classic mix of American folk rock and blues, driven by Stephen Bailey (guitar and vocals) recognised for his work as a solo artist and for his role as lead vocals and keys in psychedelic rock outfit, Mt. Mountain (Fuzz Club UK)) who have shared the stage with greats including: King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard, Thee Oh Sees, Moon Duo, The Murlocs, Orb, Sleep, The Babe Rainbow) and showcases the musical talents of local musicians: Callum Anderson (lead guitar and vocals) Noah Symmans (drums) Danny Miles (guitar and vocals) and Tom Beech (bass) Bookings For AUS/NZ: paul@billionsbooking.com.au pat@billions.com.au BOOKING AGENCY RESPONSIBLE FOR AUSTRALIA/NZ for; Amyl & the Sniffers | Angel Olsen | Basia Bulat | Bon Iver | Budos Band | The Bures Band | Car Seat Headrest | Death Cab For Cutie | Devendra Banhart | Floodlights | Gareth Liddiard | GOAT | Grace Cummings | King Gizzard & the Lizard Wizard | Lee Fields & the Expressions | Los Coronas | Menahan Street Band | The Murlocs | Pavement | Soccer Mommy | Southern River Band | Spacey Jane | Surprise Chef | Thee Sacred Souls | Tropical F**k Storm
