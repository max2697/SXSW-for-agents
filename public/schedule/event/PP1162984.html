Summary

Our students, educators, schools, and nation face a health crisis—our diets and inactivity are killing us. Over 70% of youth are unfit for military service. 71% of girls, 58% of boys don’t meet activity guidelines. By 2030, Americans will be half as active as in 1965. This Meet Up unites cross-sector changemakers ready for a Moon Shot: bold, collaborative action to improve the health of students and educators.
