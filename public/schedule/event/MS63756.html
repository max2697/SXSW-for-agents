Summary

There’s something about MURTY’s voice that sounds like a storyteller who can flirt, argue, and get in his feelings all within the same breath. He slides between smooth R&B, natural soul instincts, and unfiltered honesty. His songs feel like late-night confessions, unsent texts, and the confidence it takes to say what most people only think. He sings with intention. There’s humor in the pain, soul in the simplicity, and zero interest in staying in one lane. MURTY isn’t here to sound perfect. He’s here to sound like himself, flaws, feelings, and all. He's not just making music. He’s making moments you laugh at, relate to, and secretly replay.
