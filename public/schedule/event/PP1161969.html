Rethinking Higher Education to Build an AI-First Workforce

Event ID: PP1161969

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:15 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

AI's rapid integration risks leaving entire groups—and vast talent pools—behind. In this panel, explore the awareness gaps impacting low-income communities and actionable strategies to cultivate an inclusive, future-ready workforce. Learn how collaborations between today’s leading companies, nonprofits, and colleges/universities will prepare students for AI-driven careers. Ask important questions like, “should degrees even be required?,” hear real-world examples of employer-backed training to equip students with applied skills, and discover how initiatives like these can be replicated.
