Die Slo

Event ID: MS64113

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 9:10 PM - 9:30 PM

Venue: Shangri-La

View on Official SXSW

Summary

An underground hip-hop collective; Die Slo Entertainment, that doubles as a record label, is the combination of three Austin-based hip-hop groups who merged to work collectively as one unit. Its core members include Sertified , SouthSide Hippie, WKDZD, and Clova. With a particularly diverse set of emcees, all coming from different cultural backgrounds, Die Slo Entertainment produces equally diverse music, and are believers in the alternative and underground nature of their music, the group of hip-hop producers attempt to spread their positive motto "Stay humble but stay hungry," throughout the world.
