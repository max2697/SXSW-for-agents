Scuttino

Event ID: MS63523

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 10:05 PM - 10:20 PM

Venue: Brushy Street Commons

View on Official SXSW

Summary

"Your Favorite Cousin” Scuttino is one of the most exciting new voices in hip-hop. The multifaceted rapper, singer, and songwriter hails from Oak Cliff, a tough inner-city area of Dallas, Texas that’s also home to artists such as Yella Beezy, N’Dambi, and The D.O.C. With his love for hip-hop, neo-soul, and R&B, Scuttino has developed a versatile vibe that fuses classic instrumentation with modern production, along with his own mellow style and gift for wordplay. Inspired by a diverse range of artists, from T-Pain to Lil Wayne to hometown hero Erykah Badu, he has crafted a sound that is fresh and innovative, yet rooted in tradition and southern culture. Scuttino sets himself apart from other artists with his smooth and confident flow, his soulful melodies, and his distinctive vocal fry, which gives him a unique and recognizable voice that makes him stand out in the crowded hip-hop universe. This breath of fresh air has captivated audiences, both locally and online, with streams and views that are constantly piling up across multiple platforms. Keep your ears open for Scuttino, who's sure to take the music world by storm and become your favorite artist's "Favorite cousin".
