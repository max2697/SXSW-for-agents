Sensory Storytelling - Using XR to Connect to the Body

Event ID: PP1162632

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Touch, breath, viscera, pulsing sound - XR can be much more than a trick of the eye. Meet 4 artists developing revolutionary work supported by the UK’s Immersive Arts. Hear about their groundbreaking work that connects our bodies and senses; a multiplayer VR work redefining menopause (The Baby Factory is Closed); a multisensory dancefloor foregrounding access (Beat Blocks); explore breath as a symbiotic force fusing VR, networked performance, and biosensitive technologies (Breathscapes); and a hands on interactive sci-fi work exploring queer & post-human ecologies (Hermaphrogenesis).
