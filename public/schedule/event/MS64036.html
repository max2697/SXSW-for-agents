Summary

Alex Irish is a Texas-born artist redefining modern hip-hop through a genre-blurring sound that weaves rap, rock, pop, and Latin influences into something unmistakably her own. Rooted in Dallas, her music balances emotional depth with bold production, pairing lived experience with sharp musical instincts. After surviving a life-altering shooting in 2021, Alex turned adversity into creative momentum, channeling resilience into her art rather than retreating from it. Her latest EP "From Dallas, With Love" debuted to strong momentum, earning over 50,000 streams in its first week and charting on the iTunes Hip-Hop charts. The project also received a nomination for EP of the Year at the Dallas Entertainment Awards. Known for commanding performances and cinematic songwriting, Alex continues to carve out her own lane. With new music on the way, she represents a new chapter of Dallas hip-hop that is fearless, versatile, and unmistakably global.
