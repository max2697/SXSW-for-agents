Summary

promqueen is a queer second generation Vietnamese American Pop/Rap Artist based in Austin. Signed under mHart - Texas’ first and only Asian American music label - promqueen operates between Music, Storytelling, Community, and Identity, combining both Vietnamese and English lyrics in her songs and visuals. 2026 will feature the release of her third album, “szn three,” as well as the inaugural MODERN event: a music and fashion festival celebrating Vietnamese American lifestyle taking place in Austin in August.
