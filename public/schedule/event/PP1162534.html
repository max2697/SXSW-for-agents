Experience as Medicine: Designing Invisible Therapies

Event ID: PP1162534

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Waller Ballroom A (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

What if spaces weren’t just smart, but sensed us, soothed us, and helped us return to ourselves? This panel explores a new frontier where architecture, neurotechnology, and sensory science converge to create intelligent environments that adapt and recalibrate our mental and emotional states. From responsive hotel rooms to emotion-aware mobility, we’ll show how humanity-centred multisensory design is becoming the next layer of infrastructure for human flourishing, with real-world case studies, metrics, and ethical principles.
