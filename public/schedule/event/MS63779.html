Summary

Lodri (Rodrigo Nieves) Venezuelan Producer/Composer based in Austin, currently in his beginnings in the music industry. Actively involved in the city’s emerging music movement. His sound blends Latin rhythms with electronic influences, creating a fresh and vibrant style that represents the new wave of urban music in Austin. He has taken part in various local and events, collaborating with artists, collectives, and brands that support the independent music culture. He brings a strong sense of variety and versatility to his instrumentals. He experiments with genres such as Afrobeat, Reggaeton and Merenguetón, and has also begun exploring styles like R&B, Tech, and Dembow.
