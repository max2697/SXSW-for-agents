Summary

Here is a polished, professional version suitable for a press page: --- **DJ EXILE** has played a pivotal role in elevating Latin music within the nightlife scene of Las Vegas. As an official DJ on Jennifer Lopez’s *“It’s My Party”* tour, he brought his high-energy sound to arenas across the country, solidifying his presence on a national stage. He has also opened for and performed alongside some of the biggest names in reggaeton and Latin music, including Bad Bunny, J Balvin, and Ozuna, at premier nightlife destinations such as Drai's Nightclub, Omnia Nightclub, Marquee Nightclub, and KAOS. Touring extensively throughout the United States, DJ EXILE continues to expand his reach while staying deeply connected to his hometown audience. A proud Las Vegas native with a strong and loyal following in the Vegas Valley, he is also the Co-Founder of Altura Presents, one of the premier Latin entertainment brands in Las Vegas. Through large-scale festivals, nightclub takeovers, weekly events, and concerts, Altura Presents continues to shape and elevate the city’s Latin music culture.
