Summary

Differentchris is a first generation born and raised Houston based indie artist. His music balances vulnerability and confidence, pairing catchy melodies with introspective themes that explore identity, relationships, and personal growth. At 11 years old he began gravitating to songwriting and poetry, and knew he wanted to make music. Chris grew up listening to rock and roll with his mother and classic Norteńo with his dad, and fell in love with the structuring of songs. Known for his dynamic live performances, Differentchris has been rapidly building momentum across Texas, headlining his own regional tour and cultivating a growing fanbase through both recorded releases and high energy shows. His sound sits at the intersection of indie, alternative pop, and modern singer-songwriter music, drawing listeners in with honest storytelling and memorable hooks. With a clear artistic vision and a strong connection to his audience, Differentchris represents the next wave of Texas artists pushing indie pop forward. His SXSW performance marks a key moment in a fast-growing career rooted in authenticity, consistency, and creative evolution that he envisioned at a young age.
