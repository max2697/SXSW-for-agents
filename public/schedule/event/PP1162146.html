The Future is Low-Tech: Lessons from the Early 2000s

Event ID: PP1162146

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 9:20 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom B (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Let's rewind back to the early 2000s – slower interfaces, finite gameplay, loading bars, shared family computers and digital experiences that rewarded patience, curiosity and real-world interaction. In this talk, we'll unpack the overlooked design features of the era and explore how they can be applied today. This session is for designers, educators, technologists and decision-makers who want to take part in building a low-tech future!
