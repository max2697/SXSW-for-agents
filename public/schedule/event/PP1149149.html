Finding My Soul in Chinatown: The Making of an Accidental Activist

Event ID: PP1149149

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Grace Young—James Beard Humanitarian of the Year, never planned on becoming an activist. But when the pandemic gutted America’s Chinatowns she went from cookbook author to the unexpected frontline advocate for America’s Chinatowns. In this talk, she unpacks how personal storytelling, chance encounters, and cultural memories fueled her pivot into activism. Her story challenges you to define your own purpose—and inspires you to show up, push boundaries and take action for the communities and causes you care about.
