Neurogaming: The Future of Interactive Entertainment

Event ID: PP1162595

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

From button-mashing to brain-driven play, neurogaming is set to rewrite the rulebook. This panel hosted by OpenBCI gathers neurogamers and more to show how real-time EEG/EMG decoding replaces sticks and keys with pure intent, unlocking worlds that respond to mood, focus, and micro-gestures. We’ll spotlight a live neurogaming case study, unpack further real-world examples, and sketch the roadmap toward a brain-driven future of play.
