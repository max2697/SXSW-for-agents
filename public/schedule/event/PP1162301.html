Future-Proof Your Strategy: An AI Test and Learn Lab

Event ID: PP1162301

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Venue: Brazos I-III (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Forget fuzzy predictions. It’s time to get real. What if AI doesn’t unfold the way we expect? In this hands-on, high-energy workshop, you’ll test your strategy against four radically different futures: Growth, Constraint, Transform, and Collapse. You’ll game out scenarios, stress-test decisions, and build a future-ready playbook to lead—not just react—through AI’s uncertainty. Walk away with fresh insights, a sharper strategic lens, and tools to navigate the complexity and opportunity ahead.
