Femi Temowo

Event ID: MS63403

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 11:00 PM - 11:40 PM

Venue: Flamingo Cantina

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Femi Temowo is a MOBO Award-nominated artist, composer, and music producer. His mastery on guitar and distinctive styles quickly earned him widespread recognition, leading to collaborations with world-class artists including George Benson, Amy Winehouse, Courtney Pine, Taylor Swift, The Roots, Omar, Soweto Kinch, and many more. Beyond his virtuosity as a guitarist, Femi is a highly respected producer known for crafting warm, immersive soundscapes that blend jazz, soul, and African rhythms with contemporary influences. His production portfolio includes studio albums, live projects, and original scores for prestigious institutions such as the BBC and the National Theatre. Among his recent work, Femi has produced tracks for emerging UK artist Frenchie (formerly The Naked Eye), bringing his signature blend of musical depth and emotional resonance to new voices. Continuously evolving, Femi Temowo remains a vital creative force, shaping modern music with authenticity, cultural heritage, and innovative artistry.
