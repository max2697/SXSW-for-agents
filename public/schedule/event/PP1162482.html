Event ID: PP1162482

Summary

SailGP is transforming water into the world’s most thrilling sports arena. Founded by Larry Ellison and Sir Russell Coutts, this high-speed, data-driven, climate-positive sailing league is built for the digital age. With 60mph races in iconic cities, real-time data, and immersive broadcasting, SailGP delivers scroll-stopping content for Gen Z. It’s not just a sport—it’s a purpose-driven movement redefining how fans experience live competition and putting them at the heart of the action.
