Gen Z Professionals Meet Up

Event ID: PP1162423

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Room 408 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As Gen Z enters the workforce during a time of cultural, technological, and workplace change, the need for career-building spaces that reflect our realities has never been more urgent. The lines between brand, communications, and content continue to blur. Gen Z professionals are being asked to think holistically across platforms, communities, and culture, often without clear mentorship or support. This session will spotlight how we, as young professionals, can build meaningful careers and elevate the standard for inclusive, cross-disciplinary brand storytelling in the process.
