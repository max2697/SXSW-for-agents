DJ BONES

Event ID: MS63973

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 12:00 AM - 12:45 AM

Venue: Hotel Vegas at Volstead (Hotel Vegas)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

SP Dj/Productor mexicano y licenciado en negocios de la música que se ha consolidado como una de las propuestas jovenes mas relevantes del país; llegando a hacer mas de 120 shows al año, locales e internacionales. Actualmente DJ del artista de regional mexicano Tito Double P además de colaborar en eventos con Travis Scott, Young Miko, Cris MJ, Natanael Cano y más. EN Is a Mexican DJ and Producer with a degree in Music Business. He has established himself as one of the most relevant and with huge demand emerging talents, performing over 120 shows per year across Mexico and taking part in international tours, achieving global exposure while representing both Mexico and his hometown. He is currently the official DJ for regional Mexican artist Tito Double P, and has also collaborated on events with artists such as Travis Scott, Young Miko, Cris MJ, Natanael Cano, among others. With 11 years of experience, Bones stands out for a career defined by talent, a strong musical identity, and remarkable versatility. Musical Genres: Latin House, Afro House, Reggaeton, Latin & Club Open Format.
