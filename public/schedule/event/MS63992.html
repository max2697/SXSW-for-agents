Stockz

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 2:10 AM - 2:25 AM

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Stockz is a Houston-based artist and entrepreneur redefining what modern hip-hop can represent. Operating at the intersection of music, finance, and education, he transforms real-world experiences—risk, discipline, ownership, and long-term vision—into sharp, motivational records that resonate both on stage and off. Known for confident delivery and polished, cinematic production, Stockz has performed on major stages including Rolling Loud (U.S. & Thailand), REVOLT World, and a Live Nation–headlined show at House of Blues Houston. He has also brought his sound to academic and cultural spaces, performing at UT Austin’s Student Founder Fest and opening for national tours alongside artists from TDE and the West Coast hip-hop circuit. His live shows blend high energy with intention—equal parts performance and mindset. Beyond music, Stockz has collaborated with brands such as Nike, adidas, Experian, Samsung, True Religion, and REVOLT TV, bridging corporate partnerships with authentic cultural storytelling. A former Roc Nation–affiliated artist, he brings a rare combination of industry polish, independence, and entrepreneurial clarity to every release.
