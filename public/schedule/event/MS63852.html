Summary

Remey Williams is a 2× Grammy-nominated singer, songwriter, producer, and drummer from Camden, New Jersey, now based in Hampton, Georgia. Raised in the church and born into a musical legacy as the son of O’Jays bassist Jimmy Williams, Remey began playing drums at age 10 and developed a soulful, emotionally driven sound. He has collaborated with artists including Summer Walker, Usher, Ari Lennox, and Childish Gambino, contributing to certified projects such as Still Over It. Currently the touring drummer for Summer Walker, Remey’s work spans live performance, production, and major TV placements, including PowerBook: Ghost and The Chi.
