Summary

Armen Paul is a multi-platinum artist, songwriter and instrumentalist. His latest collaboration, "Hope" received 3x platinum certification in 2025 (#1 on national radio, #3 on UK dance radio). He performed the song in July at Tomorrowland 2023 in Belgium. Armen’s solo work took off when Bella Poarch (#3 top TikTok creator worldwide) and Tony Hawk featured his song "Devil Knows" in their social content (accumulating +175M views on TikTok). His work has received notable acclaim on DSPs with +50M streams ('New Music Friday' in +10 countries, 9 Spotify 'Viral 50' Charts). He’ll be releasing a collaboration EP produced by Moby in 2026 with a single collaboration with Don Diablo and remix from Brooks. Armen also has an upcoming single with Nicky Romero. Armen’s music has been licensed by WWE, Adobe, Lyft, and Samsung, and he secured a sync in the feature film titled 'The System' starring Tyrese Gibson & Lil' Yachty (October 2022). His recent Eurovision placement “Survivor” qualified for the 2025 Grand Final in Basel, Switzerland for +160 Million viewers, aired on Peacock globally, and reached Spotify’s Top 50 Global Viral Chart and the Spotify Viral 50 Charts in 25 Countries.
