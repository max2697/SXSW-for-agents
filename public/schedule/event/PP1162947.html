What Happens When Software Can Transact Autonomously?

Event ID: PP1162947

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 4:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Venue: Red River Ballroom (The Thompson Hotel Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Autonomous AI agents are moving beyond recommendations to making decisions and executing transactions on our behalf—reshaping the shopping journey itself. As commerce becomes software-mediated, experiences are increasingly optimized for machines, not humans, changing discovery, choice, and brand interaction. While agent-led commerce reduces friction, it raises new questions around trust and accountability. This session explores how agentic commerce reshapes incentives for merchants, platforms, developers, and consumers, and introduces the technical frameworks enabling agent-led transactions.
