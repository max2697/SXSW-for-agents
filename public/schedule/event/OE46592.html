German Music Export at SXSW: Day 5 at German Haus

Event ID: OE46592

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 2:00 PM - 12:30 AM

Venue: Speakeasy

View on Official SXSW

Summary

For the final day at the German Haus we go out with a bang. If you are a music industry professional come early for networking between 02:00 - 03:00PM. The daytime showcase starts at 3:00PM featuring Agatha is Dead!'s raw post-punk intensity, Bikini Beach's fuzz-drenched garage psych and t-low's emotional trap. For the evening we’ve curated bold and uncompromising performances: from KUOKO's dreamy indie synth-pop and Rosa Anschütz's experimental soundscapes to Modeselektor's high-energy bass-heavy beats. <br><br> For more information please visit: https://www.german-haus.com
