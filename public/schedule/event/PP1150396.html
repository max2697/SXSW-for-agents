How to Get the Job: Building a Career in Music

Event ID: PP1150396

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Capitol Ballroom FGH (Downright Austin)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Philip Payne and Sofia Martinez will reveal how they secured roles at major music companies. Whether you’re aiming for a full-time position or independent contract work, they will share the exact strategies they used to get their foot in the door. From A&R and touring to agency roles, Philip and Sofia will break down what actually work - including successful cold outreach and turning internships into careers. You’ll walk away with industry-standard networking tips and a personalized roadmap to jumptart your own career.
