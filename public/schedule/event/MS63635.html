Venue: British Music Embassy @ Palm Door on Sixth

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Queer Brighton powerpop besties Hanni (they/them) and Clara (she/her) released their second album ‘Good Boy’ in Autumn 2024. They’ve toured with MUNA, Fletcher, Yungblud and The Big Moon, and they composed several original songs performed by the cast of BBC drama Riot Women, which aired in Autumn 2025. They are currently working on their third album with longterm collaborator Steve Ansell (Blood Red Shoes), which will continue their mission to spread queer joy in 2026 and beyond.
