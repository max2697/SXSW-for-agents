Harnessing AI Without Losing Our Minds: A Playbook

Event ID: PP1162910

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Onyx Ballroom 1-4 (The LINE)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

As AI reshapes everything, how do you pivot your life, career, or company before you’re left behind? Join Jacqueline Corbelli—3x founder and investor, UN and Vatican advisor, and author of Changemaker—for a practical session on navigating inflection points. Drawing on decades of experience transforming companies, Corbelli shares proven solutions to empower individuals and companies, offering a modern framework for using AI, adopting the right mindset, and evolving in a world where five-year plans no longer apply. Are we too early, too late—or right on time?
