Global Advertising and Marketing Meet Up

Event ID: PP1150514

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Room 303 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

We could all use a little more real human connection in the creative industries right about now. This is a come-as-you-are gathering for passionate creative folk who work in advertising, marketing, or a creative field nearby. If you’re someone who makes things, shape ideas, and cares deeply about the work, you are one of us. Whether you’re here to meet new friends in the business, trade stories and insights, or let your network grow naturally, this is an ego-free zone. No sales pitches and no icky networking – just bring a coffee, a curious mind and positively lovely vibes.
