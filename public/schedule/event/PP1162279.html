4D Volumetric Video & Gaussian Splats for XR Creators

Event ID: PP1162279

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 9:30 PM

Venue: Guadalupe (Courtyard Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Step into the future of content creation with this hands-on workshop exploring 4D Gaussian Splatting—the next evolution in volumetric video. Learn how real-time volumetric capture is transforming live streaming, XR experiences, and immersive storytelling. From filming with multi-camera rigs to compressing, rendering, and deploying in platforms like Unreal Engine, Unity, SuperSplat, and UVRSE, you'll discover how this cutting-edge tech brings people, performances, and places into fully spatialized digital worlds.
