Texas Video Game Industry Meet Up

Event ID: PP1162503

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM

Venue: Brazos (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

From Doom and Age of Empires to Words With Friends and Metroid Prime, Texas has produced some of the most iconic games in the world. Home to the 3rd largest video game industry in the U.S., Texas is a powerhouse of talent — and with expanded state support, including cash grants, there’s never been a better time to work in TX-made games. Join fellow game makers and fans to explore how we can continue to grow and strengthen our industry together. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, this meet-up is your chance to network, share ideas, and celebrate everything made in Texas
