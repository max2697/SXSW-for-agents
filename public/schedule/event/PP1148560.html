The City That Plays: Digital Twins Reimagining LA & Beyond

Event ID: PP1148560

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Room 201-202 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Los Angeles is a vibrant playground for innovation where data, design, and community imagination converge. Powered by cutting-edge Earth-observing satellite systems, digital twins are transforming how LA plans for resilience, equity, and the joy of the games. Join us to explore groundbreaking projects—from wildfire recovery to creating green space networks—that leverage user-friendly immersive technology to connect AI, local stories, and spatial data. Discover how these immersive tools are shaping a smarter, more resilient city ready for the Olympics and the complex challenges ahead.
