Summary

JOPLYN is a Berlin-based electronic live act moving between melodic house, techno, and electronica, guided by a distinct voice - both literal and emotional. Writing, producing, and performing exclusively original material, her live shows unfold as immersive experiences where vocals, synths, and improvisation meet intuition and intensity. Her music has traveled widely through collaborations with artists such as Booka Shade, Röyksopp, Robyn, Damian Lazarus, MK, and Jan Blomqvist, with releases on labels including Defected, Get Physical, Diynamic, Crosstown Rebels, Mobilee, and Watergate Records. In 2022, Pete Tong named her BBC Radio 1’s Future Star. Beyond the club, JOPLYN’s work reflects a growing curiosity for depth, vulnerability, and transformation. After more than 150 shows worldwide, including Ibiza residencies at Hï Ibiza and WooMoon/Cova Santa and a debut at Coachella’s Yuma Tent, she is opening a new chapter. She is currently working on her fourth studio album, set for release in 2026 on her own label None Of The Above (N.O.T.A.) Berlin, exploring a more expansive and emotionally resonant sound.
