Exotic Thinking: Outsmarting AI with Human Creativity

Event ID: PP1162584

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

In the foresight world, staying brilliant in the Age of the Algorithm demands more than just tech fluency—it requires radical creativity. In this book reading, technofuturist Sarah DaVanzo and AI pioneer Miro Dimitrov explore how we can outthink AI using paradox, taboo, and cognitive disobedience. Drawing from Sarah’s forthcoming book Exotic Thinking, the session offers a provocative path to rewiring human ingenuity in a future shaped by machines.
