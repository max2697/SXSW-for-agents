PIAO

Event ID: MS64060

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 3:00 AM - 3:40 AM

Venue: Elysium

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Shanghai born, Los Angeles transplant PIAO is the artist you’ve been waiting for. With heartfelt lyrics and a vocal range that hits both ends of the spectrum, PIAO breaks the mold of the traditional pop artist and creates her own lane. Berklee graduate and multi-instrumentalist, PIAO's relentless pursuit of musical growth has granted her invaluable opportunities to collaborate with esteemed artists, producers, and industry giants including the likes of T-Pain, Myles William, Banx & Ranx, and more, as well as touring with notable acts like Mariana Trench, Tiffany Day, Jhariah, and more. Since releasing her debut EP, Tissues, PIAO’s music has quickly captured the attention of listeners and industry tastemakers alike. Landing on major editorial playlists like New Music Friday, Hyperpop, Next-Gen Singer-Songwriter, Jasmine, Pop All Day, Alpha, Feeling Blue, and New In Pop, PIAO’s music has amassed millions of streams and received praise for her strong melody and relatable storytelling. With new music set to release in Spring 2026, prepare to immerse yourself in the enchanting world of PIAO and experience a new era of pop music.
