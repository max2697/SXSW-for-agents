The Audacity of Joyosity™: The Surprising ROI of Joy

Event ID: PP1150040

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Depletion and disengagement are everywhere. Leaders are in crisis—decision-fatigued, over-optimized, and white-knuckling their way through the week. AI, efficiency, and metrics aren’t the answers. Joy is. Jenn Whitmer, leadership and culture expert, reveals through stories and research how joy drives performance and profit. You'll learn what joy is, why it works at work, and how to use the Joy Ratio to create sustainable results without grinding harder, toxic positivity, or lowering the bar.
