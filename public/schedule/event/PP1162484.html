Brazil Creative Economy Meet Up

Event ID: PP1162484

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Room 408 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Brazil has one of the most innovative creative economies in the world. It is influenced by its multicultural background, versatility and diversity that shape and inspire its creative economy ecosystem. Come meet some of Brazil's communication professionals, audio-visual producers, designers, publishers, brands and startups, where a fluid exchange of ideas, business models and collaborations will take place. This is a place of encounter to develop a mutual and beneficial culture of exchange between all SXSW attendees and the Brazilian creative community.
