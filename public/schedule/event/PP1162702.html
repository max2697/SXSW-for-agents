Summary

What if your dream job doesn’t exist? So you create it! During this interactive session, award-winning designer and creative educator Julie Bonner shares how she built a joyful, sustainable career by making a living from her diverse interests. Through branding, community, and creative entrepreneurship, Julie shows how to create a clear professional story and turn multiple interests into real income.
