AI & Culture: Empowering the African Diaspora

Event ID: PP1162599

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Summary

AI is transforming industries, yet cultural representation often lags behind. This session explores how AI can be used to preserve cultural heritage, amplify diverse voices, and create new business models across the African diaspora. Through real-world case studies in entertainment, fashion, and events, we’ll uncover strategies for building inclusive AI ecosystems that merge cultural identity, creativity, and innovation for global impact.
