Skills You’ll Need To Succeed in the Post-AI Age

Event ID: PP1162912

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon D (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

We’re living and working in the AI age. But you already knew that. The question is: What can you do to prepare, manage, and make the most of AI-inspired change? This session is here to give you answers. It’s based on the Skills Horizon, a dynamic guide to everything you need to know next. And it features practical insights and wisdom drawn from conversations about tech, mindset, creativity, trust, productivity, and other big topics with dozens of leaders from around the world. In just one hour, you’ll walk away ready to succeed and lead in these AI-centric times.
