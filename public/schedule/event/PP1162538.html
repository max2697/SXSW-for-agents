Urbanists and Citymakers Core Conversation

Event ID: PP1162538

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Room 410 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Urbanism, SXSW, and Austin are an inseparable pairing! In 2025 we kicked off the the urbanists meetup where dozens of architects, planners, and placemakers took a pulse of the most exciting urban ideas at SXSW. We also forged ties between SXSWers with local Austinites. The meetup features a round of self introduction, followed by some light, interactive networking activities. We will also discuss urbanism in Austin, and provide a platform for attendees to propose walks and outings to see new and historic projects that make up Austin’s urban fabric.
