Culture Meets Commerce: Building the Next $1B Brand

Event ID: PP1150561

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Waterloo Ballroom 4-6 (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Emma Grede, serial entrepreneur and multiple-time billion-dollar brand founder, joins Alison Furman, PwC’s Consumer Markets Industry Leader, and Dallas Dolan, PwC’s Technology, Media & Telecommunications Industry Leader, for an engaging panel discussion on how culture and commerce collide to create the next billion-dollar product category. The discussion will explore how cultural relevance, technology, and consumer behavior intersect to shape emerging brands, and what today’s business leaders must understand to build, scale, and sustain the next generation of category-defining companies.
