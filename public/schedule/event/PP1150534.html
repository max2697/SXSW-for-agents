Latine/x Voices in Film: Creatives Meet-Up!

Event ID: PP1150534

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Venue: Whitis (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join Cine Las Americas to celebrate local Latine/x voices in film and the vibrant creative community thriving in Austin. This is a chance to connect with international filmmakers and engage with a platform dedicated to amplifying Latine/x stories. Cine Las Americas, alongside organizations like Malakita Productions and filmmaker Alejandro Pedemonte, showcase how local talent shapes the global film landscape. Whether you're seeking collaboration, inspiration, or a pathway to share your vision, we invite you to connect and be a part of this vibrant growing community.
