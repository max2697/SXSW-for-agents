YES AND MAYBE

Event ID: MS63320

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | TBD

Venue: Swan Dive Patio (Swan Dive)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Born amidst the cultural vibrancy of Glasgow and Paris, YES AND MAYBE is an electrifying two-piece band that pushes the boundaries of contemporary music. The first band member, a multi-instrumentalist and writer, excels in crafting minimalist guitar-based melodies, demonstrating a perfect blend of ingenuity and precision. His obsessive self-criticism and penchant for perfection have allowed him to transform his influences into his own unique sound. The second member, a performer, writer, and producer, brings his experiences and emotions to life through introspective lyrics paired with evocative, ethereal vocals. His artistry lies in balancing deeply metaphorical, grounded lyrics with melodies that evoke a sense of escapism. This potent mix of realism and fantasy forms an integral part of YES AND MAYBE's distinctive sound. ​ Their songwriting process, both organic and instinctive, evolves from a simple phrase or a recorded sound into a comprehensive musical piece within a day. Their songs encapsulate their raw emotions, serving as a beacon of authenticity in an industry often mired in overproduction. YES AND MAYBE, though relatively new, brings a refreshing spontaneity and a shared vision to redefine the musical landscape. Their commitment to creating music that resonates with their listeners' shared experiences makes them a band worth investing in. Keep an eye on this Glasgow based duo—they're a unique proposition with a future as innovative as their sound.
