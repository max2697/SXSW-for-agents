Summary

REDZED is a Czech rap-metal outlier turning underground chaos into global momentum. Writing and producing everything himself — from raw guitar riffs to hard-hitting beats — he fuses serrated modern rap with the weight of late-’90s/’00s metal. The result is a dark, cinematic crossover that speaks fluent mosh pit and playlist alike. His 2024 album Tales Of A Necromancer solidified a singular vision, while viral tracks like “Rave In The Grave,” “Meth Phonk,” and “Drugs = Magic” have racked up tens of millions of streams, pushing him past one million monthly Spotify listeners worldwide. Onstage, REDZED is relentless — sweat-drenched, high-impact, and impossible to ignore. After sold-out headline shows, international tours, and major European festival slots, he arrives ready to turn SXSW into a collision of rap kids and metal die-hards. Enter the pit.
