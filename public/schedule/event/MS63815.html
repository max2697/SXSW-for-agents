Summary

Oya Noire (she/they) is a stunning Haitian-American singer and songwriter from Brooklyn, NY. An undeniable force and artist, Oya started off as a backup vocalist for 2x Grammy winner, Timothy Bloom. Now, as an independent artist, Oya Noire channels her New York City roots fusing high-energy with Neo-soul and Jazzy rhythms that linger long after the song ends. A storyteller with magnetic lyrics, Oya writes songs that are honest and unapologetically her own. She mesmerizes audiences, captivating music lovers at iconic venues such as Baby’s All Right, SOBs NY, and Blue Note Jazz Club. With her stage presence and call-and-response hooks, Oya's hit singles “Slow Down”, “Hunnid Band$”, and “Gangsta” are sure to warm up any crowd. A fan-favorite with over 5.5 million streams across DSPs and a well-anticipated mixtape on the way, Oya Noire leaves no heart untouched and no lyric wasted.
