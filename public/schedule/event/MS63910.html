Summary

Caleb Michael And The Arrested formed in 2021, but Caleb had been performing and singing for over a decade. Formed a band as quick as he could and became Caleb Michael And The Arrested. By 2022, the band was a local favorite in Denton, Celina, and surrounding areas. The crowds continue to grow and the music has become a high energy Country/ Southern Rock vibe. Headlining their first Midwest tour in Summer 2025 has raised their expectations and they have set their sights on some pretty big things in 2026!
