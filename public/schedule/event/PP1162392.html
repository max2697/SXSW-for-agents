How to Design a Successful Work Culture

Event ID: PP1162392

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon B (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Work culture gaps remain unbridgeable at many companies, yet failing to shrink them in today's multi-generational work world is like ignoring the oil light on your dashboard. You're not avoiding a breakdown—you're scheduling it for later. Join this remarkable line-up of today's top work culture experts to hear what keeps driving the divides—and how these designers are helping firms shift traditional career models, link people strategy to business value and use AI and new narratives to improve how people experience work, not as a demographic challenge, but as a design and strategy imperative.
