Summary

Born in Long Island, New York and raised in San Diego California, Drea Dominique has the flavor of both coasts. With her smooth R&B voice and fresh twist on the pop sound, Drea Dominique has what it takes to succeed and make an impact in the music world. Drea is garnering a great deal of attention with the release of her singles, "Stutter" and the remixed version with Domino Saints. They are the first of several singles coming from her upcoming project soon to be announced. Drea has also appeared in music videos for some of today’s most high-profile artists. She has done a variety of modeling work, from magazine publications to print ads. Drea is calling Miami, Florida home now and is currently introducing herself to the music industry.
