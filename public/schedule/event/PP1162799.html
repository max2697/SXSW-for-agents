The Abortion Highway: Abortion Funds & Texas Abortion Toll

Event ID: PP1162799

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Venue: Salon E (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

We dream of reproductive liberation for all communities, within Texas and beyond. Hear from Abortion Funds who advocate daily for abortion seekers and provide folks with compassionate support to make healthcare decisions that are best for them, regardless of age, income level, zip code, documentation status, or gender identity. Join us to learn how to fight back against anti-abortion policymakers and stigma strategically so we can co-create a future where all people have the power and resources to care for and affirm their bodies, identities and health for themselves and their families.
