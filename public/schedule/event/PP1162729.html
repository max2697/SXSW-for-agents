Finding Belonging in a Disconnected World

Event ID: PP1162729

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Salon D (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

In a world driven by likes, swipes, and algorithmic connection, we’re lonelier than ever. This panel—featuring leading voices in dating, friendship, psychology, and event design—unpacks how we can forge more fulfilling relationships in a tech-saturated world. Whether you're single, partnered, or building a chosen family, you'll walk away with research-backed insights, new frameworks for intimacy, and practical steps to connect more meaningfully—with others and yourself.
