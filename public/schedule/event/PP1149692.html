Building Without Permission: Democratizing Entrepreneurship

Event ID: PP1149692

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | TBD

Venue: TBD Venue

View on Official SXSW

Summary

For decades, company building required permission from institutional gatekeepers. If you didn't go to the right school or know the right people, you were locked out. AI is changing that. Just like bedroom producers no longer need record labels, solo founders no longer need MBAs or VC connections to build businesses. But access to tools isn't enough. Most still fail, not because they can't build, but because they don't know how to navigate uncertainty. This panel explores what infrastructure needs to exist for entrepreneurship to truly be accessible to everyone, and what happens when it is.
