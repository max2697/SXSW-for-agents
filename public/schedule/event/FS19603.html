Thanks for Nothing

Event ID: FS19603

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:48 PM

Venue: Violet Crown 4 (Violet Crown Cinema)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Katharina, Ricky, Malou, and Victoria live in a self-made anarchist group home - no parents, no school, no rules. Under the loose watch of social worker Ballack, things are going pretty well. But Katharina doesn’t want to live past 18, and her birthday is two weeks away. Her friends scramble to hide her suicide attempts, afraid the truth will destroy the only “home” they’ve ever had. In this darkly comedic tale of rebellion, abandonment, and surviving the absurd, "Thanks For Nothing" is the kids' collective FU to the adults who’ve abandoned or failed them.
