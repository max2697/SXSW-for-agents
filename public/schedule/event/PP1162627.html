U.S. vs. The World: the Future We’re (Currently) Losing

Event ID: PP1162627

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Salon AB (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Is the United States losing its edge in global innovation? In this high-energy continuation of last year’s SXSW session, Congressman Gabe Amo and VC Matthew Weinberg delve further into how Trump-era research cuts, clean energy rollbacks, and talent restrictions have created lasting consequences for U.S. competitiveness and opened the door for China to surge ahead. Blending policy insight with on-the-ground investor perspective, this advanced fireside chat asks: How do we revamp a system with no Plan B, and what bold, bipartisan moves can win our future back before it’s too late?
