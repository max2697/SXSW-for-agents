Summary

Maru Haru is a gaggle of musical misfits amplifying Asian American and queer experiences through bombastic anthems, intimate strings, and personal stories of heart and strife. Their projects are community driven, collaborating with multimedia artists, Asian American and LGBTQ+ vendors, and cultural centers throughout Austin, TX for not only concerts, but also films, food festivals, and celebrations. No matter the occasion, Maru Haru is motivated to share affirmation, empathy, and joy through their music.
