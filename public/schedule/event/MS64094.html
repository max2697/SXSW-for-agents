Summary

Isaac Sosa, better known as SOUCREAM, is a Venezuelan artist born on July 5, 2003. He lived in his country until 2018, the year in which he emigrated with his family to Buenos Aires, Argentina. There he discovered the world of funk and began to collaborate with Mauro Hansen, his producer and creative ally, with whom he continues to create today. In January 2023, he decided to leave Buenos Aires and settle in Miami, Florida, a city that opened the doors to accelerated growth within the Latin Funk movement, recognized “el funk de tu pais”.
