Buddy Red

Event ID: MS63173

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 1:00 AM - 1:40 AM

Venue: Saxon Pub

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Buddy Red is the Southern blues artist delivering the raw, cinematic sound of the Black American experience. A vocalist, guitarist, and preacher of truth, he stands where the cosmic mystique of Jimi Hendrix meets the uncompromising funk of James Brown and the psychedelic scope of Pink Floyd. A fixture of Atlanta's legendary Northside Tavern, Buddy is a vital voice recognized for his visceral guitar work and impassioned storytelling, captivating live and digital audiences alike. Since his breakout debut at SXSW, which included a featured set at The Shade Room's activation in 2023, his momentum is undeniable. He recently performed at the James Brown Estate for the Black Music Action Coalition’s Juneteenth celebration and shared the stage with Little Richard's original band at Tutti Frutti Music Festival. His new single, "Sold His Soul," cemented his rise as a cinematic blues revivalist, with his cultural influence extending through partnerships with the Atlanta Hawks and BMW and a festival performance at Neon Prarie in Tulsa. Buddy Red is returning to SXSW as an essential force.
