Shreea Kaul

Event ID: MS63279

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 9:40 PM - 10:10 PM

Venue: Flamingo Cantina

View on Official SXSW

Summary

India-born, Houston-raised artist Shreea Kaul bridges her South Asian roots and Western influences through music. Her viral single “Tere Bina” sparked over 100k Instagram Reels and 30k YouTube Shorts, while her directorial debut music video has surpassed 1 million views, establishing her as both a bold vocalist and visionary creator. Her previous project, “Seven Deadly Sins”, was one of her first breakthroughs in the music industry, amassing notable acclaim. This LP, much like her hit “Tere Bina”, seamlessly blends the nostalgia of early South Asian music with contemporary R&B. With her new, Grammy considered, album “Cruel Intentions”, Shreea Kaul continues to solidify her reputation as one of the most inspiring upcoming acts from the South Asian diaspora.
