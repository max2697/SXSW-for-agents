Featured Session: Why Saying Yes to the Unthinkable Works

Event ID: PP1148489

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon 6-8 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

We’re conditioned to play it safe—but what if the wild, unpolished ideas are actually the smartest bets? In this talk, you'll hear real stories of bold ideas that went from "no way" to "how did they do that?" It's a rallying cry for creators and leaders to stop self-editing, embrace bold thinking, and protect the unthinkable—because that’s where the magic (and real impact) happens.
