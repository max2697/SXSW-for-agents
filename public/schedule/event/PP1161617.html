It Takes a Region: Cybersecurity Pathways in Rural Alabama

Event ID: PP1161617

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 9:00 PM - 9:45 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

In rural eastern Alabama, a cybersecurity talent pipeline is taking root—powered by bold collaboration between K–12 schools, postsecondary institutions, and local industry. This session shares how a regional alliance is designing hands-on learning experiences, embedding aligned credentials, and unlocking high-wage, high-demand careers for students. Discover how a rural community is transforming opportunity through workforce-aligned education and community-wide vision.
