How Music with Games is Rewriting the Soundtrack of Culture

Event ID: PP1162432

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom D (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

The world’s biggest gaming platforms aren’t battlefields, they’re social networks with soundtracks players choose, share, and craft. This panel brings together innovators from STYNGR, Moonsworth (Lunar), Hangout, and the Major Labels to explore how music discovery, creator tools, and community-driven worlds are reshaping the relationship between artists, players, and platforms. Learn how mods, servers, and UGC economies are becoming the next frontier for music engagement and fandom. Join us for an in depth review of how to harness and monetize the power and cultural capital of music
