A New Performance Venture Launch

Event ID: OE46696

Fri, Mar 13, 2026 | 10:30 PM - 12:30 AM

Venue: Innovation Clubhouse (Brazos Hall)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join us at SXSW for the launch of OH.io, a new kind of performance venture for founders with traction who are ready to scale. Instead of burning months and millions trying to brute force go to market alone, OH.io takes on the cost, risk, and operational burden of building a revenue engine that actually scales, without touching a dollar of runway. Founders stay focused on the product, while we build and operate the GTM motion alongside them. OH.io combines an embedded GTM engine, a dedicated fund, and a physical operating hub called The Grid. Come meet the team on what it really takes to scale.
