Behind the Scenes: Empowering Production with AI Tools

Event ID: PP1162452

Tue, Mar 17, 2026 | 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Venue: Bridge Ballroom AB (Omni Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

While generative AI dominates headlines, the true AI revolution in film and television is happening behind the scenes. From automated script breakdowns that assist with casting, budgeting, and identifying visual effects opportunities, to real-time compliance tools for flagging mature content themes, to simplified contract review for summarizing rights and talent obligations, artificial intelligence is transforming how film and television are planned, produced, and delivered. This panel unpacks how AI is streamlining production behind the scenes and raising new legal questions.
