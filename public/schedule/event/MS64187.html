Summary

deCasa is a genre-bending duo hailing from Puerto Rico, now making waves from their home base in Austin, TX. Comprised of two brothers, the project blends the vibrant rhythms of the island with the wide-open sonic landscapes of the Lone Star State—a fusion they proudly call beach music. Seamlessly weaving English and Spanish lyrics, deCasa crafts a sound where reggae meets western twang, merengue grooves collide with funk, and cultures converge in unexpected harmony. Anchored by a powerhouse drummer whose precision and flair elevate every performance, the band delivers a live show that’s as explosive as it is unforgettable. Texas audiences have seen them light up stages from Austin’s Mohawk to San Antonio’s Paper Tiger, Dallas’ Granada, and Houston’s White Oak Music Hall. Along the way, they’ve shared bills with some of the state’s most notable acts, steadily building a reputation as one of the region’s most compelling emerging artists. With their unique cross-cultural sound, undeniable chemistry, and high-energy performances, deCasa is a name you’ll be hearing much more of—one to watch closely in 2026.
