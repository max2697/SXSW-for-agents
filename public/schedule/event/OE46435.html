Audible Presents Mexodus: A Musical Journey Across Borders

Event ID: OE46435

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 8:30 PM - 9:50 PM

Venue: Vulcan Gas Company

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Experience the groundbreaking live-looping hip-hop musical Mexodus. Created and performed by Brian Quijada and Nygel D. Robinson, this innovative work explores the untold history of the Underground Railroad that ran south into Mexico. <br> <br> Through a blend of musical influences and compelling storytelling, Mexodus brings to life the struggles and triumphs that reveal the bond between two unlikely friends. Join Quijada and Robinson as they deliver “an electrifying theatrical experience” (The New York Times Critic’s Pick) that transports through time and across borders in a concert version of “the season’s first must-see musical” (The Wrap).
