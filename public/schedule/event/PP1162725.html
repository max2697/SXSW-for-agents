No Perfect Plans, Just Progress: Austins Housing Miracle

Event ID: PP1162725

Wed, Mar 18, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Room 201-202 (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

In the last two years, Austin, TX, has implemented some of the most ambitious land use reforms in the country, unlocking new possibilities in one of the nation’s fastest-growing and most expensive housing markets. But the real story isn’t just about zoning changes—it’s about the unlikely coalition that made them possible. We will take you behind the scenes of Austin’s housing transformation, sharing how to piece together technical expertise, grassroots activism, and strategic positioning to drive major policy wins.
