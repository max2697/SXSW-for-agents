Intergenerational Workplace Culture Meet Up

Event ID: PP1149947

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Room 408 (Hilton Austin Downtown)

Summary

The stereotypes between generations at work are old and tired, and they’re getting in the way of real collaboration. This Meet Up creates space for all generations to talk openly about communication, mentorship, and what working together should look like, while also exploring “friendtorship,” a collaborative, cross-generational relationship built on authenticity, shared learning, and mutual support.
