Summary

"A Detroit native, on the rise with a mission to bridge the gap between faith-based platforms and mainstream music. Parris Chariz, short for charismatic, is an edgy, yet vibrant artist with a trap sound. Parris experienced numerous downfalls throughout his life but those valleys lead him to music. His debut album “2045” gained the attention of tastemakers within the CHH and mainstream Hip-Hop community. From being listed on Rapzilla’s Freshman in 2016 List to headlining a nationwide tour along with Ty Brasel, WHATUPRG, & Aaron Cole. Parris has made his mark within the Hip-Hop community that is extremely unique and far from the rest. His music has encouraged his listeners to know what’s real and to be true to yourself through every obstable this life brings us. His recently released album “Take You To Parris” has set a standard of authentic creativity provided a voice to the unheard. Parris has made his mark as a creative and will continue to make music that speaks to the soul."
