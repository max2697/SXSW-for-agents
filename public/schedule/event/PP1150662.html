Summary

Join the Guardian’s Football Weekly hosts Max Rushden and Barry Glendenning, alongside Senior editor, soccer Alexander Abnos, for a special live podcast recording. Football Weekly, the #1 soccer podcast, offers a uniquely balanced format - insightful enough for devoted followers, yet accessible and entertaining for casual listeners. Expect the show’s signature take on this year’s World Cup, the biggest global soccer tournament ever. Plus the biggest stories in soccer all brought to life on stage with lively debate, funny stories, and audience interaction.
