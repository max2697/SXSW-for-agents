Global Impact Founders Meet Up

Summary

Join Valentina Primo and Victoria Kuketz, two Obama Scholars advancing cross-sector social impact, for intimate conversations on what it really takes to build initiatives that move the needle. The meetup aims to bridge foundations, corporations, and institutions driving social impact across different levels, from tech innovations, to AI policy, to investors backing the bold ideas shaping the future. Expect sharp questions, real-world insights, and a forward-looking take on what’s next in innovation.
