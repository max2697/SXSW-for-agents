Summary

Afroheart prodigy, Dayonthetrack was introduced to the world in November 2019, he featured on Groundup Chale tape titled “We Outside; Y3 W) Ab)nten vol 1” where he featured on three songs off the project.   After the EP, he made remarkable strides in songwriting. Writing back to back hits for including Baajo, Celebrate and toxic by Kwesi Arthur writing parts of Adekunle Gold’s lyrics on the latter. He also featured on two songs off the “Son Of Jacob” album which has been exceptional on streaming markets and gained nominations on prestigious events.   His debut EP -SOUNDS AND WAVES released in July 2023 have earned Dayonthetrack over two million streams on all platforms as a solo artist project and his "Ashawo" song off the project being his first solo hit song.
