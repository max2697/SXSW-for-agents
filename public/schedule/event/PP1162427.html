Your First 90 Days as a Creator from Experts at Meta, YouTube & Snapchat

Event ID: PP1162427

Sat, Mar 14, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Waller Ballroom C (Austin Marriott Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

In this rare cross platform conversation, moderated by acclaimed creator strategist Antonia Alakija, creator partnerships leaders from Meta, YouTube & Snapchat come together to break down what creators should focus on in their first 90 days of taking content seriously. We’ll explore how fast growing creators structure early momentum, the common pitfalls that derail progress & strategies for building an audience with intention. From platform-specific insights to proven growth frameworks, this session offers an honest tactical roadmap for turning creative potential into a lasting personal brand.
