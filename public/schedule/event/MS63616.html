Summary

An artist at the crossroads of country and hip-hop, Shai Gabriel creates the kind of songs that feel as epic as a movie: gripping, unpredictable, immense in scope but rooted in raw emotion. On his journey, the Texas-bred singer/songwriter charts a cinematic road trip through the wilds of the American South, bringing a powerful new depth to his storytelling while pushing further into the country/folk-inspired sound he's explored in recent years. Drawing inspirations from Country, Pop, Hip Hop and Gospel he creates a dynamic sound that captures the hearts of fans.
