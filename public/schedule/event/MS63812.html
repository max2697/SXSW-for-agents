Summary

John Robert Hill Jr. (b. August 3, 1996), better known by his stage name Holy Gabbana, is a rapper coming out of Jacksonville, Florida. In December of 2018, John was sentenced to 5 months in county jail and had a life changing experience. He began to grow an understanding of Jesus Christ due to his cellmate, which ultimately led him to getting saved and starting a Christian hip hop career. He released his first single as a Christian rapper, “Holy Flow” in August 2022.
