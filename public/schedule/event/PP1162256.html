Operating Systems of the 3rd Millennium: AI + XR + NEURO

Event ID: PP1162256

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM

Venue: Salon D (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Neural interfaces are entering a new phase where AI algorithms read brain signals in real-time and power adaptive feedback loops. This panel convenes OpenBCI and cross-disciplinary experts to show how AI turns raw brain signals into actionable insights, powers real-time closed-loop haptics and visuals, and scales from lab demos to consumer and research-grade neurotech. We’ll dissect technical hurdles, safety imperatives, and the roadmap to trustworthy neurotechnology.
