Summary

Darkovibes is a boundary-pushing Ghanaian singer, songwriter, and genre-bender whose sound blends Afrobeats, highlife, R&B, and alternative influences into a unique musical identity. A pivotal voice in the new wave of African music, he has collaborated with some of the continent’s biggest stars, including Wizkid, Davido, Shatta Wale, R2Bees, Sarkodie, Stonebwoy, Camidoh, King Promise, M.anifest, Mr. Eazi and Spinall. Known for his vibrant performances and versatility, Darkovibes has shared stages with many of these artists across Africa and beyond, captivating audiences with his infectious energy and bold creative vision. With a growing global fan base and a track record of innovative releases, Darkovibes continues to redefine contemporary African music and is poised to make a powerful impression at SXSW.
