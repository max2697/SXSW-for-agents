Sober Space Meet Up - 3/15

Event ID: PP1149774

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Venue: Brazos (JW Marriott)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Sober Space is a welcoming gathering place for people in all forms of recovery during SXSW 2026. This space hosts all-format recovery meetings for Friends of Bill and all SXSW attendees who are seeking a supportive meeting environment during the festival. Participants are invited to sit together and share experience, strength, and hope in a confidential, judgment-free setting. Meetings are facilitated by The SIMS Foundation alongside trusted leaders from the recovery community.
