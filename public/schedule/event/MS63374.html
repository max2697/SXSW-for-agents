Les Itinérantes

Event ID: MS63374

Mon, Mar 16, 2026 | 2:40 AM - 3:10 AM

Venue: Flamingo Cantina

View on Official SXSW

Summary

One of France’s most distinctive a cappella acts, Les Itinérantes turn three voices into an immersive, ritual-like live experience. Les Itinérantes (french for "The Wanderers") are one of France’s most acclaimed a cappella trios, recognized for transforming vocal performance into a powerful live experience. Founded in Paris in 2017, the group was born from a chance encounter and a shared desire to explore the voice beyond established genres. Without instruments, they approach singing as a living, embodied material - a space of memory, transmission, and human connection. Their repertoire spans over nine centuries of music and more than fifty languages, blending ancient and traditional songs with original compositions. This diversity is not encyclopedic, but intentional: a search for what unites people across time, cultures, and borders. Praised by the French press for the ritual, purity, and emotional depth of their performances, Les Itinérantes are known for rare vocal precision and a strong sense of presence. Their concerts are intimate and immersive, offering audiences a focused, shared experience of listening.
