Stories from a Second Chance

Event ID: PP1162352

Sun, Mar 15, 2026 | 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Venue: Salon A (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

Join author Jesse Crosson for a special first-public reading of his forthcoming memoir, (publishing March 10, 2026). In this session, Jesse will read select passages that dive into his journey and how the hard-won lessons he learned apply to your life as well! He will discuss how accountability is the pathway to freedom, how service is redemption in action and how building a life of options is much more successful than building a life of plans. This is a rare chance to hear these stories live and unfiltered, straight from the place where transformation began. Pre-order the book now and be among the first to hear the journey from darkness to rebirth—on stage at SXSW. https://www.jessecrosson.com/my-book/
