Who’s Funding the Future—and Who’s Getting Left Out

Event ID: PP1162633

Thu, Mar 12, 2026 | 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM

Venue: Salon D (Hilton Austin Downtown)

View on Official SXSW

Summary

While billions flow into tech, too little reaches the people building for equity, justice, and long-term public benefit. This session brings together leaders rethinking who gets to fund emerging tech and why—across both venture capital and philanthropy. We'll explore how to back founders closest to the problems, build alternatives to extractive tech, and redirect capital toward innovation that actually serves communities. It's time to expand who defines innovation—and who profits from it.
