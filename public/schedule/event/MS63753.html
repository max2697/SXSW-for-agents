Summary

Kuala Lumpur-based rapper, singer, and songwriter Lil Asian Thiccie has earned herself a spot in the forefront of the Malaysian urban music scene by continually pushing the envelope. With a lustful sound that teases trap, pop and everything good in between, her tongue-in-cheek lyrics and dreamy delivery marks her distinct sound and persona. With an established career and presence as a standout female rapper and hip- hop starlet that has spanned the last four years, Lil Asian Thiccie is now introducing a new era of her music and identity. With an all-new sound and direction, this release plan covers the journey ahead for Lil Asian Thiccie as she evolves into the hyperpop superstar of her dreams.
