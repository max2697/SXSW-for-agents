Summary

Monsieur Van Pratt, founder of Super Spicy Records, is a prominent producer and DJ originally from Mexico City, whose influence on electronic music is widely recognized both nationally and internationally. With an impressive career that included more than 100 releases in digital format and 30+ on vinyl, which consolidarse him as a key figure in the Disco, Funk, and House scene. His music has been released by prestigious record labels such as Toy Tonics (coming in 2026), Too Slow To Disco, The Disco Express, Tropical Disco Records, Buddha Bar, his own label Super Spicy Records, among others, underlining his status on the global scene. With a musical style deeply rooted in Disco, Funk, and Jazz, Monsieur Van Pratt combines classical elements with contemporary touches, creating a sound experience that is both nostalgic and innovative. His work not only stands out for its technical quality, but also for its ability to connect emocional y with the listener, making him a central figure in the evolution of modern dance sound.
